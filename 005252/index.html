Variable Objects and Truthmaking
Friederike Moltmann
March 2016
 

This paper argues that definite NPs like 'the number of people that can fit into the bus', 'the book John needs to write', and 'the gifted mathematician John claims to be' stand for variable objects, objects that may have different manifestations in different situations and whose definition involves the notion of truthmaking. These objects are part of the 'shallow' construction-driven ontology of natural language, yet they are real.
