An overview of Mayan phonology, syntax, and semantics
Ryan Bennett, Jessica Coon, Robert Henderson
May 2015
 

This is a combined pdf of three introductory articles on Mayan languages covering phonology, syntax, and semantics. The work is aimed at non-specialists, especially those needing a first stop before digging into the wide literature on Mayan languages. The articles will appear in Language and Linguistics Compass along with a short introduction that we have not included here.
