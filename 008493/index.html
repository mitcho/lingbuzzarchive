Number agreement of coordinated subjects: Competing syntactic and semantic rules
Manfred Krifka, Fereshteh Modarresi
October 2024
 

We investigate number agreement in coordinated subjects, including disjunctions and conjunctions of singular universal quantifiers. Experimental data from German reveals that they often allow for singular agreement. We argue that there is a competition between semantic and syntactic agreement, and that the latter is a generalization of the former.
