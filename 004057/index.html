Economy, Verb Second, and the SVO-SOV Distinction
Hans-Martin Gaertner, Markus Steinbach
January 1994
 

Among many other things, this paper contains an "early" discussion of the relevance of the German "tun"-Periphrase ("do-support"?) for the position of I°. Also, the ban on fronting (non-interrogative) "wh"-indefinites is pointed out as a phenomenon favoring symmetric approaches to V2.
