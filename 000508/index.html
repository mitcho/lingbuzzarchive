On EPP effects
Halldor Armann Sigurdsson
June 2010
 

In this paper I argue that EPP effects are of two different types, NP-movement vs. the Filled Left Edge Effect. While NP-movement is driven in Narrow Syntax by the computation of Person, some Filled Left Edge phenomena, including Icelandic Stylistic Fronting, seem to take place in PF. Even so, these phenomena also reflect the clausal computation, that is, the matching processes that value clause internal elements in relation to the linguistic context, above all features of the Speech Event.
