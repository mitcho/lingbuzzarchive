The Mysteries of Bilingualism (book review)
Norbert Francis
November 2022
 

In the science of language, as in all of its sister disciplines, a mystery is an empirical question yet to be resolved. The research problems surveyed in this book offer students of bilingualism and second language learning a guide for designing their own research program, one that keeps an eye on the pending questions. Practitioners will be well served by the review of recent work that updates ongoing projects from years past. Issues in Applied Linguistics, 23(1), 22-29 (2022).
