Predicate movement in Dutch and Hungarian
Hans Broekhuis, Veronika Hegedus
December 2007
 

This article argues that the preverbal placement of Small Clause predicates in Dutch as well as the placement of the so-called verbal modifier in Hungarian follows straightforwardly from the Broekhuis' (2005) reformulation of Hoekstra and Mulder's (1990) analysis of English locative inversion (see lingBuzz/000154).
