Review of Applied Translation Studies
Norbert Francis
April 2018
 

Translation studies is an important part of the broader field of bilingualism and language contact. This book by T. K. Lee brings forward the relevant questions for this branch of Applied Linguistics. Readers will be interested to learn about recent debates and controversies.
