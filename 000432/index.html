Prism of Grammar (MIY Press 2007): Extensions
Tom Roeper
April 2007
 

"Prism of Grammar" MIT Press, April 2007 has just been published. It is a semi-popular book that focuses on acquisition, dialects, and intellectual responsibility. It contains new material on how Merge works, recursion, ellipsis, and the acquisition of quantification. Three chapters on passive, sequence of tense, and recursive possessives had to be omitted. They are now published here.
