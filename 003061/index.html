Where is the Dutch Stress System?
Marc Van Oostendorp, Bjoern Koehnlein
July 2016
 

We tested some of the predictions of standard Dutch stress theories against the data from a few experiments involving nonsense words. The results give new evidence that the stress system is indeed quantity sensitive in a rather sophisticated way; but we did not find evidence for some other claims, such as the existence of a 3-syllable window in Dutch.
