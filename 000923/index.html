Slovenian Clitics Have No Unique Syntactic Position
Franc Lanko Marušič
January 2008
 

The aim of this paper is to show (partially following Bo¹koviæ 2001) that Slovenian clitics do not have any unique syntactic position. Further, I will show that the presented data also pose a problem for Bo¹koviæ’s (2001) proposal for second position clitics.
