On a Focus Particle Analysis on Cantonese Wh-NPI matzai
Margaret Chui Yi Lee
August 2023
 

This paper examines the semantics of Cantonese wh-NPI matzai ‘(not...) that much’. It first studies the semantics of each component of the wh-NPI, namely the wh-indeterminate pronoun mat ‘what’ and the excessive modifier zai ‘excessive(ness)’ by looking at their distributions. It, then, proposes a novel compositional account which illustrates the contributions of mat and zai for the overall semantics of the wh-NPI. The current proposal requires a permissive modal component which allows us to pin down a contextually determined standard for comparisons between the actual value and the expected value. Lastly, a pragmatic enrichment operator is necessary for deriving the polarity sensitivity, which explains the negation requirement of matzai.
