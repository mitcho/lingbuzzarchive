A homosyllabary list of Yuanling Dujiaping dialect in Hunan Province
Huang Hongjiang
July 2023
 

Yuanling County (沅陵县) in Hunan Province (湖南省) is situated within an area of mixed contact among the Xiang Dialect (湘语), Southwest Mandarin (西南官话), and Xianghua (乡话). Rich types of dialects are attested in this county. This article provides a homophony syllabary for the rural dialect recorded in Dujiaping Township (杜家坪乡) of Yuanling.
