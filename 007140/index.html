A (Very) Brief Comparison of Bengali and English Phonological Systems
Man Ho Wong
February 2023
 

In this paper, I compared the similarities and most notable differences in phonology between standard forms of Bengali and General American English, including consonants, vowels, syllable structures, and prosody. This paper was originally submitted as a term paper for my phonetics course at the University of Pittsburgh. The speech sound analysis involves a speaker of a West Bengali dialect. I decided to upload it here because I think this dialect might be of interest to researchers of the dialect. Besides, a direct comparison of Bengali and English phonology might be helpful for L2 learners of Bengali or English.
