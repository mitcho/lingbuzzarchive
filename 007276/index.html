Hypothesis on Root Language
Zhidong Lu
May 2023
 

The hypothesis on Root Language suggests that there is a unified, implicit language underlying human thinking, which can be referred to as the root language. This language operates solely on the level of thought and is projected into various real languages through a language conversion mechanism. The hypothesis proposes that exploring this language's underlying structure, specifically its internal presentation form of thinking, can provide new theoretical support for a professional-class machine translation system.
