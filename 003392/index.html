The achievements of Generative Syntax: a time chart
Roberta D'Alessandro
April 2017
 

This is an updated version of the time chart on the results of generative syntax that I had drafted for a position paper I'm preparing. I took a tentative list from Peter Svenonius's blog, reporting on the Athens meeting from 2015, and tried to put the achievements on a time chart. This updated version includes some suggestions, and many corrected dates, and has been drafted with the help of many people. Comments/additions/corrections are very welcome! Thanks!
