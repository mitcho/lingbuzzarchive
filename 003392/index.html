The achievements of Generative Syntax: a time chart
Roberta D'Alessandro
July 2019
 

This is an updated version of the time chart on the results of generative syntax that I had drafted a while back. It includes the comments of several scholars, including 3 reviewers. I took a tentative list from Peter Svenonius's blog, reporting on the Athens meeting from 2015, and tried to put the achievements on a time chart. Then I drew some considerations. This updated version includes some suggestions, many corrected dates, and has been drafted with the help of many people. Comments/additions/corrections are always very welcome! Thanks!
