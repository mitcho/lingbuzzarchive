Perfective Auxiliary Selection in heritage Italo-Romance
Luigi Andriani, Roberta D'Alessandro
December 2023
 

These are some notes on auxiliary selection in some Northern and Southern Italo-Romance varieties, based on our fieldwork in the NYC, Argentina and Brazil. The data are very few, but we can still see some general trends worth investigating.
