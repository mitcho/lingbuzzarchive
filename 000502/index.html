Liquids in Cuban Spanish
Grisel Maria Garcia-Perez
August 2006
 

The study analyzes to what extent certain constraints of the Cuban Spanish dialect spoken in Havana can be explained in the light of the theory of generative phonology. A series of phonological rules describing the processes which characterize this variety of Spanish are presented.
