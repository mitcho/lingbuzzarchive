A bottom up approach to category mapping and meaning change
Eitan Grossman, Haim Dubossarsky, Chris Dyer, Yulia Tsvetkov
March 2015
 

In this article, we use an automated bottom-up approach to identify semantic categories in an entire corpus. We conduct an experiment using a word vector model to represent the meaning of words. The word vectors are then clustered, giving a bottom-up representation of semantic categories. Our main finding is that the likelihood of changes in a wordâ€™s meaning correlates with its position within its cluster.
