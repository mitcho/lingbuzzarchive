Strict Logophors in Ewe, Yoruba, and Igbo
Itai Bassi, Imke Driemel, Abigail Bimpeh, Silvia Silleresi
February 2023
 

We offer a solution to a semantic puzzle regarding the reference profile of logophoric pronouns: on the one hand they must be internally bound to an attitude holder, on the other they do not have to be bound to it for the purpose of the `strict/sloppy' ambiguity. Our solution is based on the novel idea that the relationship between an attitude holder and a logophor is encoded at the presuppositional level of meaning and does not require formal binding (see also Bimpeh et al 2023).
