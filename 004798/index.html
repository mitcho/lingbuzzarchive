On the Usage of the Paradigm 'brauchen' with and without 'zu' in an Infinitive Construction
Luciano Melodia
May 2015
 

In this study we use machine readable syntactic annotation to isolate paradigms of German written language. Using pattern mining the paradigm 'brauchen' with 'zu' and without the particle in an infinitive construction shall be investigated. Statistical methods as well as machine processing methods are used to isolate the paradigm within different newspapers of the German-speaking area. We present our results and interpret them according to the question which variety can be understood as standard.
