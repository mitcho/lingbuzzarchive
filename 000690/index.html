A Note on Targets of A-movement in the Left Periphery of German Sentences
Josef Bayer
January 2006
 

Considering the split-CP hypothesis for German has initiated a rethinking of traditional assumptions about the left periphery of the clause. The paper gives evidence in favor of the split-CP hypothesis while on the other showing how to avoid problems concerning the operator status of moved elements and weak crossover which were thought to emerge from the split-CP approach in combination with scrambling as A-movement.
