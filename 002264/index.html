From non-finite to finite subordination. The history of embedded clauses
Júlia Bácskai-Atkári, Éva Dékány
May 2014
 

In this chapter we survey the principal changes that took place in the history of Hungarian embedded clauses. We will argue that finite subordination took over non-finite embedding alongside with the development of a functional left-periphery, that is, the CP-domain of finite embedded clauses.
