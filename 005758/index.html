Negative polarity additive particles
Andreea Nicolae
November 2020
 

Many languages have pairs of additive markers that exhibit a common morphological core. This paper focuses on the Romanian pair "si" and "nici" and offers an analysis that derives their distribution and interpretation. The crux of the analysis is the claim that "nici" spells out the negative marker N and the additive particle ADD; N is argued to contribute the negative polarity component while ADD is assumed to make the same contribution as the positive particle, "si".
