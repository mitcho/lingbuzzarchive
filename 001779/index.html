Stripping and topless complements
Susi Wurmbrand
April 2016
 

In this squib I show that the combination of a certain view of ellipsis and a particular approach to the structure of embedded root clauses makes a prediction about the presence of complementizers in elliptical structures—specifically in stripping contexts—which surprisingly is correct.
