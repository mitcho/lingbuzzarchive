The Syntax of Scottish Gaelic
David Adger
October 2022
 

A brief sketch of the clause structure of Scottish Gaelic, arguing that the apparently quite diverse syntactic structures seen in the language sould be understood as being all built on the same basic structure, with two subject positions, two object positions, and a predicational core.
