The Theory of Syntax and the Representation of Indexicality
Alessandra Giorgi
September 2008
 

In this paper I propose that the left-most position in the Complementizer layer works as the syntactic interface with the context. In particular, its left-most position encodes the information concerning the utterance event itself, i.e., the speaker's coordinates. This property might be taken to be the motivation of the phase nature of the C node.
