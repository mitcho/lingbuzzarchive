Towards an Etiology of Weak Necessity in a Bengali Modal Based on Homogeneity
Shrayana Haldar
October 2024
 

In this paper, I argue that the meaning of weak necessity in the Bengali modal "hoy" arises only in its bare habitual form, but not in its other forms, where only strong necessity arises; and a clue to why this happens becomes apparent when we observe that there is something common to bare habituals and WN, that is, homogeneity. This paves the path forward towards compositional analyses of weak necessity.
