Syntax and Symmetry
Michael Brody
April 2006
 

In general it is the departures from symmetry rather than the symmetries that are in need of explanation. Hence (validly) eliminating asymmetry from the theory is ipso facto making the theory more explanatory. However, if such an approach to syntax is taken seriously to its logical conclusion, it is in apparent opposition to all minimalist type structure building, which invariably involves a(nti)symmetrical concepts like labelled merge, spec-head, head-complement, probe-goal,c-command...
