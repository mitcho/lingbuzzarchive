Clarifications on the no half-Merge fallacy
Pedro Tiago Martins, Cedric Boeckx
September 2020
 

Berwick & Chomsky (2019) raise a number of issues with Martins & Boeckx (2019). In what follows, we will briefly outline and dispel these issues, as well as clarify some points which have been the object of misrepresentation.
