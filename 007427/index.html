Voice and VP-ellipsis revisited
Elise Newman
April 2023
 

This squib observes that Voice mismatches under VP ellipsis (cf. Merchant 2013) are sensitive to the number and type of arguments selected by the verb. It argues that we can maintain Merchant's insights about VP-ellipsis and clause structure if "VP-ellipsis" can target constituents of different sizes in different structural contexts.
