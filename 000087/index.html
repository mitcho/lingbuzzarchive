The Morpho-Syntax of Expressive Derivations in Japanese: Subject Honorification and Object Honorification in Distributed Morphology
Mark Volpe
October 2007
 

Like diminutives in other languages, Japanese honorifics are argued to be "lexical", e.g., Sells (1995). This paper argues for a DM approach to Subject and Object Honorification.
