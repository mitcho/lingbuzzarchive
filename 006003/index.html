about The language Instinct -2
William Abler
May 2021
 

Plagiarism is the only crime for which evidence accumulates rather than deteriorating. As much as Thucydides, plagiarism is permanent literature. I don’t know how many fundamental laws of nature are discovered in a century, but the theft and misrepresentation of one of them extends deeper than the words, and even the ideas, to the life and soul of the discoverer.
