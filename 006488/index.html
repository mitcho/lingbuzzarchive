Russian Modernism in the Memories of the Survivors: The Duvakin Interviews
Norbert Francis
January 2022
 

This book review belongs to the series Science of Art. The interview with Roman Jakobson, in particular, sheds new light on the work of the Russian Formalists, and one of the tendencies among the Futurists. Other recollections of informants touch on interesting aspects of poetics. Readers can find previous articles in: https://ling.auf.net/lingbuzz/003616, https://ling.auf.net/lingbuzz/006335, and https://ling.auf.net/lingbuzz/006448.
