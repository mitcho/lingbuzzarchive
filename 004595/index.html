Proceedings of Form and Analysis in Mayan Linguistics 5 (FAMLi 5)

This document publishes papers from the proceedings of the 5th meeting of Form and Analysis in Mayan Linguistics (FAMLi 5), which took place Aug. 10-11, 2018 in Antigua, Guatemala. We thank the UMD Language Science Center and Guatemala Field Station and Cooperación Española for providing financial and logistical support for the conference, and all of the conference participants who helped make the event a success.
