Interacciones en el filo oracional medio: Doblado de clíticos y movimiento-A
Pujalte Mercedes, Andrés Saab
September 2017
 

In this paper, we discuss certain type of interactions between doubled objects in Río de La Plata Spanish. Based on considerations involving WCO effects, we argue that indirect and direct doubled objects are the surface reflex of syntactic A-movement to the vP edge. A new observation is that there is a ban on double doubling. This is shown with new data involving interactions between doubling and dislocated constructions.
