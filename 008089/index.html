Review of:: Steven Franks. Syntax and Spell-Out in Slavic. Bloomington (IN): Slavica Publishers, 2017
Anton Zimmerling
April 2024
 

This a review of: Steven Franks. Syntax and Spell-Out in Slavic. Bloomington, Indiana: Slavica Publishers, 2017. 346 p. I discuss the advantages and problematic points of Franks' account of Slavic clitics, Spell-out and Movement and mention several conditions relevant for clitic-internal ordering in Balcanic Slavic.
