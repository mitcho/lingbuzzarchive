Predicatives and the Predicates of States in Russian [Предикативы и предикаты состояния в русском языке]
Anton Zimmerling
August 2024
 

This paper discusses the syntax and semantics of Russian constructions with non-agreeing predicatives. I show that the constructions N DAT ― V LINK ― PRED (dative-predicative structures , DPS) and N NOM ―V LINK ― PRED are used to express the meaning of internal D-states — i.e., stage-level predicates linked to an animate subject. Pospelov's claim that all predicatives are non-agreeing elements is correct, but Russian supports the regular homonymy of predicatives vs. adjectival elements.
