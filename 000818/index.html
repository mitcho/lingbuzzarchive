Multiple NP split: A Distributed Deletion analysis
Dennis Ott
May 2008
 

This paper investigates multiply split NPs in German, where part of an NP is scrambled into the Mittelfeld while an even smaller subpart is topicalized. This hitherto neglected phenomenon is shown to involve A-bar movement (like regular split topicalization), and an analysis in terms of PF deletion (building on Fanselow & Cavar's 2002 theory of "Distributed Deletion") is developed.
