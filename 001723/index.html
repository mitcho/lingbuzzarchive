Czech numerals and no bundling
Pavel Caha
January 2013
 

The paper argues that Czech numerals spell out a phrasal projection that has a noun at the bottom. I argue that this proposal sheds light on three aspects of their peculiar syntax: (i) the case that we find on the counted noun (ii) the ambiguity between a numerical and nominal syntax (for some of them), and (iii) the fact that they may move across the demonstrative (with a partitive effect).
