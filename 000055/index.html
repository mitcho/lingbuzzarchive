Optimal gaps in optimal paradigms
Curt Rice
November 2004
 

The paper explores phonologically motivated gaps in paradigms. An analysis within McCarthy's optimal paradigms theory is suggested, which allows elimination of previous, less-satisfying OT treatments of this type of ineffability.
