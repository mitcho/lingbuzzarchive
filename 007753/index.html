Review of Salles (2023) on Pirahã Grammar
Daniel Everett
December 2023
 

This is an excerpt from a larger work in progress on the conceptual foundations of linguistics. It offers a fairly detailed and negative review of a descriptive summary of Pirahã grammar, Salles (2023). There will likely be revisions to this as the larger work of which it is a part moves towards completion.
