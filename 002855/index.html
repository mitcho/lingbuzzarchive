Notes on insertion in Distributed Morphology and Nanosyntax
Pavel Caha
September 2016
 

This paper considers two questions that many people ask themselves (or should ask themselves). What is actually the difference between Nanosyntax (NS, Starke, 2009) and Distributed Morphology (DM, Halle and Marantz, 1993)? And which one of them is right?
