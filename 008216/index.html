Lexical Decomposition of Verbs and the Notion of an Abstract State
Friederike Moltmann
June 2024
 

In this talk I argue that the notion of an abstract (or Kimian) state plays a role not only in explaining the Stative Adverb Gap, but manifests itself also in a particular constraint on explicit-property-referring terms ('the property of being wise'). I argue that those terms involve a notion of a property that consists in the HAVE-relation toward a trope-like entity or other object and is thus distinct from the notion of an abundant property, which has generally been taken to be involved in that construction.
