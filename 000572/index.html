An historical perspective on syntactic trees
Dany Jaspers
November 1999
 

This paper presents a historical overview of the two basic rule types of generative syntax, i.e. rewriting rules (Merge) and transformations (Move). I show that whereas in the Syntactic-Structures-to-GB-era the two rule types were kept distinct, in current Minimalist theorizing the re-introduction of Generalized Transformations has led to a near-complete unification of Merge and Move.
