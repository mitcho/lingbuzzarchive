Possessed numerals in Ch'ol
Carol Rose Little
August 2018
 

In this paper, I investigate a set of underdescribed numeral constructions in Ch’ol that I call "possessed numerals," due to their possessive morphology. I provide evidence that these numerals are definite and maximal and propose a semantics for them. I discuss extending this semantics to other strong quantificational constructions.
