Meaning in the Wild [Chapter 1 of "What it All Means", 2022]
Philippe Schlenker
September 2023
 

This is the preprint of Chapter 1 of “What it All Means”, MIT Press 2022. This chapter, intended for a general audience, discusses recent advances in research on primate communication, including from a linguistic perspective. Several cases of meaningful primate calls and gestures are summarized. One possible case of a monkey suffix is discussed (-oo in Campbell’s monkeys), as well as possible instances of implicatures in monkey calls (Titi monkeys, Campbell’s monkeys). An example of call preservation over millions of years is discussed (Campbell's monkeys’ boom), and recent advances on the evolution and meaning of ape gestures are summarized.
