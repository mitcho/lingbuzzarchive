Stylistic Fronting in corpora
Halldor Armann Sigurdsson
August 2017
 

This paper studies the distribution and frequency of Icelandic Stylistic Fronting in two written language corpora, Timarit.is and the World Wide Web. The survey indicates that that SF is on the retreat, losing ground to verb-initial order (V1) in relative clauses and to expletive-initial order in impersonal clauses. An encouraging extra result of the study, a methodological byproduct, as it were, is that it shows that Google Search, if carefully used, is a much more valuable research tool in linguistics than commonly assumed.
