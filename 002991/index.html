“Some Thoughts on One and Two and Other Numerals”
Richard Kayne
May 2016
 

Phrases of the form ‘numeral + noun’ never involve direct merger of numeral and noun. In every case, derivations are more complex than that. With 'one', there is, in addition to a classifier, the necessary presence of 'single'/'only', whether pronounced or silent. With 2-4, coordinate structures are involved. With 5 on up, silent SET is necessarily present.
