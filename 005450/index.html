Videos: Presentation of autosegmental phonology
John Goldsmith
September 2020
 

I have posted three videos on autosegmental phonology. These could be useful for a phonology class or seminar. They’re on my Youtube channel, https://www.youtube.com/user/JohnAGoldsmith/videos. The first presents the arguments for autosegmental representation, the second looks at two systems briefly, and third is a much deeper look at the tone system of Tonga.
