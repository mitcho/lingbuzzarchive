Natural Language Ontology - Handout 9-10
Friederike Moltmann
December 2022
 

These are the last two handouts of my fall 2022 course on natural language ontology (http://www.friederike-moltmann.com/teaching/course-fall-2022-natural-language-ontology/). Here we critically discuss Chomsky's challenges for referentialist semantics, Pietroski's elaboration of a nonreferentialist semantics, and an ontological alternative based on Fine's notion of a variable embodiment.
