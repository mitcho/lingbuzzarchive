Subject control predicates and the movement analysis of gapping
Luis Vicente
May 2008
 

Johnson (1996, 2006) proposes to analyze gapping as ATB verb movement, contrary to the traditional wisdom that took it to be a subcase of deletion. This squib shows that gapping of subject control predicates raises serious difficulties for Johnson's approach. The conclusion is that a movement analysis of gapping cannot be maintained.
