Minimalism and the Parser: Part I
Iain Mobbs
June 2011
 

This is a (very full) hand-out of a talk given at the Syntax and Biolinguistics Cluster, University of Cambridge, 20th June 2011. The work discusses several aspects of the relationship between the parser and the Minimalist Program.
