NON-FINITE VERBS IN BIRBHUM BANGLA
Srijani Mondal
May 2024
 

A non-finite verb is a verb that is not finite. Non-finite verbs are not inflected for grammatical tense and they have little inflection for other grammatical categories. Formally, they lack the three grammatical features [mood, tense and voice] that are associated, independently or relatively with the act of predication. The non-finite forms have [-tense], [+aspect] and [+/-agreement] features.
