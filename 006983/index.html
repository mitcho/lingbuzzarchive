Natural Language Ontology - Handout 8
Friederike Moltmann
December 2022
 

This is the eighth handout of a course taught in fall 2022 on natural language ontology. The topic of this session a core-periphery distinction that appears essential for the pursuit of natural language ontology. It also discusses a particular challenge to it, complex property-referring terms of the sort 'the property of being wise', which should belong to the periphery, yet display particular semantic constraints that appear to be part of core ontology, not being acquired through exposure to experience or naive or philosophical reasoning.
