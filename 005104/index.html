The Syntax of Numeral Expressions
Youri Zabbal
May 2005
 

This paper explores the internal syntax of complex numerals and how cardinals and ordinals could be built up from the same numeral system. The claim is that complex numerals are formed in the syntax using a multiplicative structure and an additive structure, in line with Hurford (1987, 2003), before combining with the noun phrase and that an analysis such as Ionin and Matushansky (2004) which conflates numerals with their function as cardinals runs into syntactic and semantic issues.
