On Mates' Puzzle
Andrés Soria Ruiz
January 2020
 

The purpose of this paper is to explore how to extend the concept generator technology devised by Percus and Sauerland 2003 to account for Mates’ puzzles, that is, sentences where co-intensional expressions cannot be substituted salva veritate. These sentences are interpreted as reporting de re beliefs about specific expressions of a language, and the concept generator technology is deployed to derive such interpretations compositionally.
