Lexicon Version 1. Manuscript MS408.
Gerard Cheshire.
June 2020
 

This is the first lexicon of words from Manuscript MS408 (Maria/Voynich). It provides definition variants for all words used in the Plant Series Papers, Nos. 1-10. The language is a form of Iberian Vernacular Latin (Proto-Romance), most similar to Portuguese, Galician and Catalan.
