In defense of the reflexivization analysis of anticausativization
John Beavers, Andrew J. Koontz-Garboden
October 2012
 

Horvath and Siloni (2011) argue against an analysis of anticausativization as reflexivization as presented especially by Koontz-Garboden (2009) for languages in which anticausatives exhibit overt reflexive marking.
