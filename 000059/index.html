Extending and Reducing the MLC
Winfried Lechner
May 2003
 

By eliminating redundancies between Merge and Move, the paper arrives at (i) a common analysis for Merge over Move effects and Case Freezing and (ii) a new, local algorithm for the computation of Superiority.
