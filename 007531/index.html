Defectivity and auxiliary syncretism
Dalina Kallulli, Ian Roberts
August 2023
 

We argue for the existence of neutralised (i.e. featurally underspecified) auxiliaries, and consequently that the feature content of auxiliaries can change over time in such a way that it interacts with the labelling algorithm.
