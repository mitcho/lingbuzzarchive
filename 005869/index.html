The Pattern Hypothesis of Voynich Manuscript
Pardis Motiee
April 2021
 

The medieval codex of voynich manuscript, had challenged people for decades. The language in which it is written, doesn’t show much similarity to the natural languages and many believe the book is a cipher. Although mystery remains, after a few observations of different patterns , there is a new path to think about the code aspects of the writings.
