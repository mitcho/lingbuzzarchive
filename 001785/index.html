Adjectival escapades
Zeljko Boskovic
March 2013
 

I examine a number of syntactic and semantic issues regarding adjectives, including Slavic adjective-like elements like demonstratives and possessors, which include word order in the traditional NP (TNP), adjective-stranding ellipsis, adjectival complements, and the effect of agreement on left-branch extraction. While I focus on Slavic, other languages (e.g. Chinese, Turkish, Japanese, German) are also discussed.
