Acceptability judgments in sign linguistics
Vadim Kimmelman
June 2019
 

This chapter is an overview of how acceptability judgments are currently used in sign linguistics, why more methodological research is needed, and how and why using acceptability judgments for sign languages might be different from spoken languages.
