Wh-islands in degree questions
Marta Abrusan
April 2011
 

It is proposed that wh-islands with degree questions are unacceptable because they cannot be given a most informative true answer. Wh-islands thus are shown to be similar to other cases weak islands which have been argued to result from Maximization Failure, in particular negative islands (cf. Fox and Hackl 2007).
