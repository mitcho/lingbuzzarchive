Peirce's Journey to the End of Inquiry: The Tenure of the Soul
Daniel Everett
February 2022
 

The purpose of this piece is to give a brief background of the life of C.S. Peirce, founder of Pragmatism, Logic, Semeiotics and much more of relevance to modern linguistic theory. Peirce is relatively unknown to many, even though they (often unknowingly) work with concepts he first developed.
