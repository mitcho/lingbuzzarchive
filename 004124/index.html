Latin/Romance finite and non-finite complementisers
Keith Tse
December 2012
 

The Romance finite and non-finite complementation system bears close resemblance to the famous examples of Germanic/English that-complementiser and to/zu-infinitive, a comparison of which reveals new evidence for the diachrony of the Latin/Romance complementisers as well as robust trends in the cross-linguistic distribution of grammaticalization.
