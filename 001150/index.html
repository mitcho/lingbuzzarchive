Prosody of Scrambling
Svitlana Antonyuk, Roksolana Mykhaylyk
February 2010
 

In this paper we discuss a production experiment that addressed the question of whether prosody and object scrambling function as the two alternative and licit ways of expressing object semantics in Ukrainian. The results of the experiment clearly support the hypothesis, suggesting that there is in fact no true optionality.
