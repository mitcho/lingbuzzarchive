Minimality Effects in Agrammatic Comprehension
Nino Grillo
May 2005
 

A new approach to agrammatic comprehension in Broca’s aphasics is proposed that provides a link between processing based and representational approaches to the topic. The central claim is that the latest formulation of Relativized Minimality (Rizzi 1990, 2001; Starke 2001) can provide such a link and make it possible to explain some well acknowledged asymmetries in agrammatic comprehension.
