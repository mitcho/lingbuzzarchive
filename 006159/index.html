Commitments and Beyond. [Comments on an article by Bart Geurts]
Manfred Krifka
July 2019
 

The article discusses the notion of commitments for the analysis of speech acts like assertions. It argues against intentions (of the speaker that the addressee comes to believe a proposition) as the primary meaning of assertions, and derives such intentions from an analysis of assertions as expressed commitments by the speaker. It discusses speech act adverbials that modify the commitment and the communicative function of commitments to private believes. It also touches on the expression of emotions.
