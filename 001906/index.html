Precedence in Grammar
Zoe Belk, Ad Neeleman
September 2013
 

We explore whether the observation that in head-initial noun phrases APs surface closer to the noun than PPs should receive a structural explanation or an explanation in terms of linear order. Our assessment is that the data support a linear account.
