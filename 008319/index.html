Coordination and Comparatives
Friederike Moltmann
August 1992
 

My dissertation explored the syntax and semantics of a three-dimensional approach to coordination. It focused on spit antecedent constructions such as 'John praised and Mary criticized the same poet', which are analysed in terms of implicit coordination (of 'praise' and 'criticize' as well as of 'John' and 'Mary'), a notion available only within three-dimensional syntax.
