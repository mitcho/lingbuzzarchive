Syncretism and Iconicity in Icelandic Noun Declensions: A Distributed Morphology Approach
Gereon Mueller
April 2004
 

The main goal of this paper is to provide a reasonably comprehensive account of the core system of noun inflection in Icelandic. The analysis is based on a decomposition of case features and inflection class features, and it crucially relies on principles developed in Distributed Morphology, viz., impoverishment and fission
