Predicate Connection and Informational Oddness
Diego Feinmann
November 2022
 

In this short paper, I present a set of minimal pairs that calls into question all current accounts of contradictoriness and redundancy. In addition, I present two generalisations—grounded in the novel notion of predicate connection (Feinmann 2022)—that provide a fresh perspective on these phenomena.
