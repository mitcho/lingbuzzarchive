Filling gaps with Glue

Lexical Functional Grammar currently lacks an analysis of gapping at the syntax-semantics interface. The aim of this paper is to fill this gap. We present two approaches that do not assume empty constituents: one employing standard Glue Semantics but relying on a particular approach to event semantics, and another compatible with any approach to semantics but relying on a particular approach to Glue Semantics at the syntax-semantics interface.
