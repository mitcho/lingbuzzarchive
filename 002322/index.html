A note on someone (else): An island repair solution and its competitors
Andrés Saab
December 2014
 

It is shown that sluicing sentences involving the unexpected absence of "else" modification for the wh-remnant (Barros 2012) are best analyzed as instances of island repair under ellipsis, specifically, as repair of the Coordinate Structure Constraint. Two alternative analyses are discussed, namely, Barros' solution in terms of a predicative strategy and a potential mutual entailment approach à la Merchant 2001, and it is shown that they have to face serious empirical challenges.
