Transfer and Dynamic Access
Jae-Young Shim
June 2022
 

In minimalist theorizing, the operation Transfer has long been assumed to contribute to reduction of computational complexity by removing from the derivational workspace the syntactic objects in the phase interior. This paper, however, addresses some critical problems with this â€˜eliminative' Transfer resulting from its stipulative nature. To resolve the addressed problems, this paper adopts the suggestions in Chomsky (2021, Forthcoming) and presents an alternative called Dynamic Access (DA) according to which the interpretive systems directly access the derivation at the phase level.
