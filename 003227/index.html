The scope of nominal quantifiers in comparative clauses
Rick Nouwen, Jakub Dotlacil
December 2016
 

We show that the interpretational effects of nominal quantifiers in comparative clauses differ from those of modal quantifiers in comparatives. We argue that nominal quantifiers take scope at a much higher level than previously assumed.
