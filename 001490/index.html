Zibun revisited: empathy, logophoricity, and binding
David Y. Oshima
April 2012
 

In past studies, several authors have proposed dichotomic analyses of the Japanese anaphor zibun, based on the anaphor/logophor or local/long-distance distinction This paper demonstrates that zibun has three distinct uses, viz. anaphoric, empathic, and logophoric (and thus a two-way distinction is not sufficient), and discuss the licensing conditions for and distribution of each use.
