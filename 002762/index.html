Review of Vyvyan Evans, "The Crucible of Language"
Geoffrey Sampson
November 2015
 

Vyvyan Evans's book appears to be a good place for outsiders to learn about the fairly recent school of "cognitive linguistics". The case he makes, however, is less than persuasive in a number of different respects.
