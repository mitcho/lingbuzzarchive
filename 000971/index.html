Expletives on the interface: Linearization and PF output conditions
Mark de Vos
November 2009
 

This paper argues that English there-type expletives are a function of bare output conditions at PF interacting with AGREE. Specific and extra-linguistic conceptions of interface conditions such as linearization and locality yield a situation where AGREE sets up a linearization paradox where an optimal solution requires displacement. In this way, movement obtains without recourse to EPP.
