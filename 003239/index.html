The third reading of the most expensive photo of Abby
Zheng Shen
August 2014
 

Superlative expressions like 'John bought the most expensive photo of Abby' has been observed to involve multiple interpretations cross-linguistically: 1) the absolute reading (ABS), 2) the relative reading with NP external focus (REX), and the relative reading with NP internal focus (RIN). This paper focusses on the recently discovered interpretation RIN. The availability of the interpretations varies across languages (see Pancheva and Tomaszewicz 2012), for example, the interpretation in (1c) is not available in the English sentence in (1) while it is available its the similar sentences in Polish. Previous researches on the RIN (Pancheva and Tomaszewicz 2012, Shen 2014) focus on the cross-linguistic aspect of its distribution but a small amount of constructions . This paper will investigate the availability of the RIN in more constructions in English and German. A new generalization of its distribution will be proposed which will be deduced with the standard locality constraints.
