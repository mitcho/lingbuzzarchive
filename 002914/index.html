To Be or To Not Be: A Moving Story about Sentential Negation
Elise Newman
February 2016
 

In this paper, I argue that there is a simple explanation for puzzling facts about the English auxiliary system if we adopt 1) a generalization of EPP, 2) Matushansky’s (2006) view of head movement, and 3) the possibility that feature movement is available as a last resort when head movement fails.
