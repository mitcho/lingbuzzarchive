Is the mapping to PHON complex?
Hisao Tokizaki
March 2020
 

Chomsky, Gallego & Otto (2019) argue that the mapping of syntactic structure to PHON (phonology) is complex compared to mapping to SEM (semantics). This article argues that the mapping of syntactic structure to PHON is not complex because it is governed by specific rules and mechanisms.
