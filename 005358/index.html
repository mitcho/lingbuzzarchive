Voynich codex; the Indian origins
Pardis Motiee
August 2020
 

A manuscript was found in ltaly, 19th century, which has amazed people for decades. The voynich contains of plant illustrations and Italian style drawings. It must be a plant medicine book according to the illustrations. Although many people tried deciphering it, the meaning behind the words still remained mystery. Mostly because we need to find what are the alphabets first. The period manuscript was written could be from 14th to 15th century.However,the letters did not look like the languages used in that time. As l looked into ancient alphabets, specially Indian ones such as Brahmi, Kharosthi and Gupta; the similar signs were recognizable. I want to describe the possibility of how the manuscript can be in Indian languages in brief.
