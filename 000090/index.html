Directionality and locality in allomorphy: a response to Adger, Bejar and Harbour
Andrew Carstairs-Mccarthy
March 2005
 

Adger, Bejar and Harbour (Transactions of the Philological Society 101 (2003))) criticise Carstairs-McCarthy (henceforth C-M) (2001b) on several points. I argue that most of ABH's criticisms are mistaken. However, they make an interesting proposal concerning locality that deserves further investigation.
