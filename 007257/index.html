The Armenian dialects of Tigranakert/Dikranagerd and Urfa
Bert Vaux
January 2006
 

This chapter treats the varieties of Armenian formerly spoken in Edesia/Urfa/Urha, Tigranakert/Diyarbakır, and neighboring Armenian villages including Xoyt', Ozni, İrinĵ, and Qıt'ırbıl.
