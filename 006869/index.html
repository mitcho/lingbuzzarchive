Small clause predicates and sluicing
Richard Stockwell
March 2022
 

This brief report bears out a prediction of Anand et al.’s (2021b) syntactic identity condition on sluicing. Identity is calculated over argument domains as small as small clauses. With extraction of a small clause subject, sluicing is possible where only a small clause predicate has an antecedent.
