Human Languages Are Constructed Languages
Patrik Austin
December 2017
 

This thesis discusses and presents evidence that "natural" languages are social constructs with no human-specific cognitive psychological or other guidance; instead, the only possible scientific view is that they are based on logic and teleology. The PhD includes a CMM to validate classical functionalism - work in progress.
