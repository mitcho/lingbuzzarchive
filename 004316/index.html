The Concept “Sadness” and its Metaphorical Models in the 21st Century American Fiction
Olha Shumeiko
September 2018
 

The article identifies metaphorical expressions underlying conceptualization of the negative emotive concept “sadness” in American prose literature. The analysis revealed twelve metaphorical images considered as figurative constituents within the structure of the negative emotive concept “sadness” in modern American language.
