On Affixal Articles: An Argument from Bantu for Bulgarian, Romanian, and Icelandic
Zeljko Boskovic
January 2018
 

The paper argues against the N-to-D movement analysis of article affixation in the N-D word order in Bulgarian, Icelandic, and Romanian based on these languages not displaying a locality effect that is attributed to N-to-D movement in Bantu languages.
