Aspectos da Fonologia do Pirahã
Daniel Everett
January 1990
 

This is the first detailed study of Pirahã phonology. It is incomplete in many respects, but it does discuss intonation at the discourse and sentence levels, deriving mid-tone, and stress placement for the first time.
