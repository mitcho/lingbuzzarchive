The Basic Elements of Inflection: Morphophonology of Bosnian Nouns
Nicola Lampitelli
January 2012
 

In this paper, I deal with the morphophonology of Bosnian nominal declensions. I propose a new insight into the behavior of the vocalic alternations occurring throughout the system. In particular, I show that the final vowel of each Bosnian noun results from the combination of three distinct phonological items, and that these items are the exponents of one and only one morphosyntactic property. These exponents are the basic elements of a Bosnian noun and are combined in one complex marker in the phonology.
