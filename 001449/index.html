Nasal Place Assimilation
Abdoulaye Laziz Nchare
January 2012
 

This paper discusses Shupamem data on nasal place assimilation using Padgett's (1995a)Feature Geometry. It also explores the Optimality Theory to account for the phenomenon of NPA. I explore the facts involving Voicing, Place, and Stricture.
