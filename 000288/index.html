Some Preliminary Comparative Remarks on French and Italian Definite Articles (2nd draft)
Richard Kayne
May 2005
 

Superlatives, bare plurals, bare mass nouns and certain interrogatives partially cluster together across Romance languages in a way that sheds light on the syntax of definite articles.
