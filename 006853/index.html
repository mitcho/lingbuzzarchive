Elliptical 'why not'
Richard Stockwell
June 2022
 

This paper investigates clausal ellipsis with 'why not'; e.g., "Chris didn't come, but I don't know why not." Elliptical 'why not' requires a negative antecedent, involves negative concord, and is subject to a clausemate condition. These empirical traits might be captured by analysing elliptical 'why not' as a version of polarity ellipsis. Despite consisting of 'not' and 'why', 'why not' behaves very differently from both negative stripping and 'why'-stripping.
