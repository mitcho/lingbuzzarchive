Gapping in Turkish
atakan ince
February 2008
 

The aim of this study is to contend an analysis of (forward) Gapping in Turkish in the Minimalist Framework, as in (1) (see also Hankamer 1972, Kornfilt 2000). (1) Burak kütüphane-ye gitti, Mustafa (da) hastane-ye gitti. library-dat went also hospital-dat went Burak went to the library and Mustafa to the hospital. I argue that gapping in Turkish consists of elision of only a matrix AgrSP. It cannot occur in non-matrix structures because these non-matrix structures lack a Co with the relevant [+E(LLIPSIS)] feature to license elision of a site within their own projections.
