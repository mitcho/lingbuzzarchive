Convergent Evidence for Rolling Up Catalan Adjectives
Andrew Ira Nevins
June 2010
 

Northeastern Central Catalan data allow one to use both PF and LF criteria in order to determine the c-command and spec-head relations in the syntax. Postnominal adjectives are argued to be derived by roll-up movement, rather than head-movement or adjunction.
