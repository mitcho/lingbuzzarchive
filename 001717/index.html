Partial pro-drop as Null NP Anaphora
Pilar Barbosa
April 2011
 

This paper argues that the same general mechanism underlies subject pro-drop in partial null subject languages and discourse pro-drop languages, namely null NP anaphora, as originally proposed by Tomioka (2003) for discourse pro-drop.
