On an undocumented type of predicate ellipsis in Spanish and its consequences for the theory of ellipsis licensing
Andr√©s Saab, Laura Stigliano
September 2023
 

In this paper, we discuss a type of ellipsis in Spanish, undocumented in the previous literature, which we will refer to as Predicate Phrase Ellipsis (PredP-Ellipsis), and its consequences for the theory of ellipsis licensing. PredP-Ellipsis, is a type of ellipsis in which the complement of a copular verb undergoes deletion, whenever there is polarity focus on the copular verb.
