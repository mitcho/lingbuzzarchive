On the cognitive evolution of grammatical subjects
Hubert Haider
January 2024
 

[typo-corrected version] Ironically, one of the very few shared assumptions between Functionalist Typology and Generative Grammar turns out to be untenable, namely their 'Agent'-based concept of grammatical subject. This paper outlines the evolutionary emergence and further development of grammatical subjects.
