Semantic of poetical tropes: Non-Fregeanity and paraconsistent logic
Basil Louri√©, Olga Mitrenina
February 2015
 

A new semantic theory of poetical tropes is proposed. It is based on the Hintikka-Sandu's theory but put into the frame of paraconsistent logic. It is shown, meanwhile, that already Hintikka and Sandu left the soil of the Fregean semantic of Montague and David Lewis and, unnoticedly, shifted to the situational semantic.
