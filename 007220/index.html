If it works for English, it must work for all languages. Or does it? Syntax and the Bender Rule: Focus on Adjunct Control
Youssef Haddad
April 2023
 

It is not an uncommon practice among linguists, mainly those of us who work on English and, on occasion, other European languages, to present their theories without stating the language they analyze in the title of their work. They present their theories as universal systems that may work for any language. This manuscript calls for change of this practice.
