In defense of Verb-Stranding VP-Ellipsis
Andrew Simpson
May 2022
 

This short paper presents arguments against the recent prominent challenge to verb-stranding VP ellipsis/VSVPE made in Landau (2020), which draws on Oku (2016), and defends the VSVPE hypothesis, arguing that it remains the strongest hypothesis available to account for many instances of ellipsis with null objects.
