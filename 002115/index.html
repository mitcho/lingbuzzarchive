Can a (Phase) Head Undergo Interpretation along with Its Domain?
Yusra Yahya
June 2014
 

In this paper, I propose that a phase head, mainly v*, belongs to its domain for spell-out purposes contra Chomsky (2000, 2001 and subsequent work). However, whether the phase head undergoes interpretation along with its complement or not is something that depends on the spell-out instruction that this phase head itself may instantiate.
