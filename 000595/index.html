On agreement restrictions
Roberta D'Alessandro, Susann Fischer, Gunnar Hrafn Hrafnbjargarson
February 2008
 

This is the introductory chapter to a collection of papers on agreement restrictions. It provides a short overview of the main phenomena addressed in the book (PCC, anti-agreement effects, agreement restrictions with Nominative objects, etc.) and a short summary of the contributions.
