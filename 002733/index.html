The actuation problem
George Walkden
March 2014
 

This chapter explores Weinreich, Labov & Herzog's (1968) 'actuation problem' in the domain of diachronic syntax, and gives an overview of the related issues of explanation and prediction that arise for any approach to diachrony. It will appear in the Cambridge Handbook of Historical Syntax, edited by Adam Ledgeway and Ian Roberts.
