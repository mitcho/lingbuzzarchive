Toward Explaining the Head-Final Filter
Dorian Roehrs
March 2010
 

This short squib discusses the Head-Final Filter, a restriction on the order of adjectives and their modifiers inside the DP. The crucial idea to capture this contraint is the assumption that the inflection is in the highest head of the extended projection of the adjective and that the adjectival stem and its modifiers move to the Specifier of the inflectional head.
