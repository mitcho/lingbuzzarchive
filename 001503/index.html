Indefinite Demonstratives in Yiddish
Dorian Roehrs
May 2012
 

This paper examines two nominal constructions in Yiddish: ‘epes a khaver’ (some friend) and ‘eyner a khaver’ (a certain friend). Discussing five different potential accounts, it is proposed that ‘epes a’ and ‘eyner a’ form an indefinite demonstrative structure in Spec, DP, where ‘epes’ and ‘eyner’ are in the specifier of ‘a’.
