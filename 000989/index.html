Overt evidence from Left-Branch Extraction in Polish for punctuated paths
Bartosz Wiland
November 2009
 

The paper argues that subextractions of the left-branch wh-phrase which strand the NP in fronted positions (also in the upper clause) provide new evidence for successive-cyclic movements in syntax.
