The nanosyntax of Path
Juan Romeu
May 2013
 

In this paper I examine the notion of Path and its presence as a projection in the structure. I give evidence that there is no projection Path as such in the structure. The notions related to Path (‘sequence of points’ and ‘directionality’) are obtained by different means.
