A Restructuring Analysis of Uyghur Bleached V2 Constructions
Alexander Sugar
June 2018
 

This paper examines multi-verb constructions in which the final verb has been semantically bleached in the Turkic language Uyghur. Along the lines of Cinque (2003) and Fukuda (2012), I use passivization data to provide a monoclausal analysis in which the final bleached may realize one of two different functional heads along the same clausal spine: the little v head which introduces external arguments, or a higher Aux head. Depending on the position of the bleached verb, the -(i)p suffix which follows the lexical verb in lieu of finite inflection realizes either an Inner Aspect head within the verbal domain or an Event head outside the verbal domain.
