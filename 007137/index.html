Pokémon corpus
Andrew Lamont
February 2023
 

Corpus of the currently 1,008 Pokémon I put together for a phonology course. Feel free to use, modify, and share!

Data scraped primarily from Bulbapedia (https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Main_Page); English IPA from the Pokémon Let's Play Wiki (https://pokemonlp.fandom.com/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Pronunciation_Guide)

Contains:
- Pokédex number
- Name in English, Japanese, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Korean, and Mandarin
- Phonological transcription of English
- What each Pokémon evolves from/into
- Its evolution level
- Whether it is a baby or a legendary Pokémon
- Its types
- Weight, height, and in game statistics

The web-scraping script is available here: https://colab.research.google.com/drive/1vekysUBVjsNENpyysaYb2xe-0TFQp-As?usp=sharing
