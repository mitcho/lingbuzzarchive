Question Particles and Disjunction
Karattuparambil Jayaseelan
April 2008
 

In many languages the disjunction marker is also a question particle. It is argued that in these languages, what superficially functions as a question particle is the lexical realization of the disjunction operator. If we now say that the disjunction marker is a copy of the disjunction operator, we have an explanation of the homophony of the disjunction marker and the question particle.
