The Latin origins of Romance prepositional infinitives
Keith Tse
September 2012
 

While prepositional infinitives are paramount in Romance, they are virtually non-existent in Latin, which leaves a gaping hole in the scholarship on Latin/Romance historical syntax. This paper considers the derivation of Latin/Romance infinitives and proposes Latin origins for certain types of Romance prepositional infinitives.
