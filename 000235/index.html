Dual is Still More Marked than Plural
Andrew Ira Nevins
June 2007
 

Cowper (2005) claims that the plural category of number is more marked than dual, on grounds of explaining syncretism in Zuni pronominal forms. This claim goes against a wide range of typological, diachronic, and developmental evidence for the markedness of dual. However, CowperÂ’s argument conflates markedness of a category with the notion of specification of vocabulary items. On a classical understanding of markedness, even the Zuni facts can be interpreted as evidence for the markedness of dual.
