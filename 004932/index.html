Sentence Processing & Syntactic Theory
Dave Kush, Brian Dillon
December 2019
 

This is a chapter for the Blackwell Companion to Chomsky. It provides some discussion of Chomsky's influence on the field of sentence processing, focusing on a handful of example phenomena.
