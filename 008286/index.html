Topic and focus asymmetries in Yorùbá
Daniel Aremu
January 2024
 

The paper discusses some notable asymmetries between topic and focus constructions in Yorùbá. Adopting the criterial approach (Rizzi 1997, 2006, Rizzi and Shlonsky 2007, Shlonsky 2014, i.a.), I argue for a movement and base-generation analyses for focus realization, and a purely base-generation analysis for topic realization in the language. I claim that the presence and distribution of resumptive pronouns in both A'-dependencies are connected to the presence of subject criterion.
