Review of Recursion Across Domains, edited by Luiz Amaral, Marcus Maia, Andrew Nevins, and Tom Roeper, Cambridge University Press, 2018.
Daniel Everett, Edward Gibson
July 2019
 

This is a review article on the book, Recursion Across Domains, edited by Luiz Amaral, Marcus Maia, Andrew Nevins, and Tom Roeper, published by Cambridge University Press, 2018. It looks closely at the evidence adduced in the book, experimental and descriptive, regarding recursion in Pirahã.
