What is a possible phonological rule?
Fred Mailhot, Charles Reiss
April 2004
 

Through an examination of vowel harmony and similar phenomena, we attempt to define a lower boundary on the computational power of phonological rules. We formalize locality and discover that the conditions in rules interact in interesting ways. We also provide a unified analysis for neutral vowels in harmony processes. The focus on computation allows for simplification in models of representation. This is an extended version of a paper that appeared as F. Mailhot and C. Reiss.
