Stress-by-Reduplication in Los Silos Spanish
Alejo Alcaraz
November 2015
 

This piece of work mainly focuses on some exotic morpho-phonological processes –at least regarding Spanish dialectology- found in the verbal compound forms of Los Silos Spanish (LS Sp).
