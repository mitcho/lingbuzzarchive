Present indicative auxiliaries in Zamudio: An Appendix to Obliteration vs. Impoverishment in the Basque g-/z- Constraint
Karlos Arregi, Andrew Ira Nevins
November 2006
 

This is an appendix to Arregi and Nevins 2006, a paper on the g-/z- Constraint in Basque (lingBuzz/000280.) We provide a detailed analysis of present tense auxiliaries in Zamudio, one of the Bizkaian Basque varieties discussed in that paper.
