Whispered Speech in Shanghainese
Xiaoya Wang
August 2023
 

Shanghai Chinese is a Wu dialect, also known as Shanghainese and Shanghai Wu, mainly spoken in and around one of the largest cities in China - Shanghai, where 24 million people reside. Shanghai Wu is an underresearched language than other tonal languages in China (e.i. Mandarin, Cantonese). The present study aims to investigate the phonetic properties of Shanghai Wu with a concentration on breathy vowels, and how they are produced in the speech mode of whisper compared to normal speech, more importantly, the cue realization and cue weighting in whispered speech as cross-linguistic phenomena.
