A note on consonant strengthening at both edges in Toda
Elango Kumaran
June 2023
 

Toda (Dravidian) strengthens consonants at both left and right prosodic edges (Emeneau 1984), which challenges the hypothesis that languages can only do one or the other (Nasukawa et al. 2019).
