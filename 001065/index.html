The Different Determiner Systems in Germanic and Romance
Dorian Roehrs
June 2010
 

Focusing on the Germanic and Romance languages, this paper discusses some differences between articles and demonstratives with regard to syntactic distribution and morphological transparency. These differences are argued to explain the absence of certain patterns in demonstrative-reinforcer constructions.
