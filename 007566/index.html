Ambiguity in high/low construal: an Agree-based approach
Qiuhao Charles Yan
August 2023
 

In this paper I present an Agree-base approach to the phenomenon known as ambiguity in high/low construal (Geis 1970, 1975), arguing against the conventional Move-based one (Larson 1987, 1990, Haegeman 2009, 2010, 2012).
