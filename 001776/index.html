Structural Decomposition of Spatial Adpositions
Peter Svenonius
November 2012
 

This is the text of a talk presented at a conference in Bochum in 2012. It motivates an approach to the syntax-semantics interface on the basis of a close analysis of spatial adpositions and path expressions in several languages.
