Head Movement as a Phonological Operation
Christer Platzack
September 2010
 

In this paper I firstly establish a diagnostics of Head Movement (HM) that helps to distinguish HM from other instances of movement, and secondly, I present a purely phonological account of HM, that circumvents the technical problems facing syntactic HM. As indicated in the paper, my account is supposed to handle the data at least as well as the syntactic accounts, without the theoretical drawbacks.
