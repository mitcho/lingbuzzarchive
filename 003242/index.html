'The Number of Planets', a Number-Referring Term?
Friederike Moltmann
December 2016
 

The paper argues that Frege was mistaken in taking 'the number of planets' to be a term referring to a number as an abstract object. 'The number of planets' either functions as a term standing for a number trope, the manifestation of being eight in the plurality of the planets, or else it is the subject of a specificational sentence and does not function as a referential term at all. The latter is the case in 'The number of planets is eight'. In a specificational sentence, 'the number of planets' stands for the question 'how many planets are there', not involving reference to a number object.
