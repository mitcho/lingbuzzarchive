Outline of an Object-Based Truthmaker Semantics for Modals and Attitude Reports
Friederike Moltmann
February 2020
 

Against the background of standard possible-worlds semantics, this paper outlines a truthmaker semantics of attitude reports and modal sentences based on an ontology of attitudinal and modal objects and on a semantic function of sentences as predicates of modal and attitudinal objects. The semantics provides a more fine-grained, situation-based notion of content and allows for a novel account of connections between modals and attitude verbs, weak and strong permission, response stance verbs, factive verbs, and harmonic modals.
