Metalinguistic ellipsis: playful silence in adverts, titles, and slogans
Richard Stockwell
April 2022
 

In playful contexts, conditions on ellipsis can be metalinguistically flouted to memorable effect; e.g. "It is. Are you?", "Yes We Can", "Everyday vehicles that aren't." This contribution surveys how and to what extent adverts, titles and slogans manipulate and thus reveal conditions on ellipsis, covering recoverability, ambiguity resolution, argument structure alternations, antecedent containment, (ad)nominal antecedents, and contrast.
