Vis-à-vis Voynich.
Gerard Cheshire.
December 2021
 

The Manuscript and the meandering Mind. Academia Letters. This article answers frequent inquiry about how the solutions were found for the writing system and language of Medieval manuscript MS408, as described in the peer-reviewed Romance Studies journal paper titled: The Language and Writing System of MS408 (Voynich) Explained: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/02639904.2019.1599566 As finding the solutions was largely intuitive, then explaining how it was done is something of a challenge in and of itself.
