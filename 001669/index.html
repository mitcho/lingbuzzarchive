Coherent Fieldwork
Daniel Everett
November 2004
 

This paper lays out a pragmatist view of field research that predates my Current Anthropology paper (2005) on Piraha culture and grammar, laying a partial philosophical and practical basis for that type of research. It points to a few surprising cases of culture and grammar from the work of Sapir which may serve as potential counterexamples for some of the statements on morphological universals recently presented in Cinque (2012).
