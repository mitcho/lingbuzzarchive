Weak-Cross Over, Scope and Agreement in a Minimalist Framework
Pierre Pica, William Snyder
January 1995
 

Our paper presents a novel theory of weak crossover effects, based entirely on quantifier scope preferences and their consequences for variable binding. The structural notion of 'crossover' plays no role. We develop a theory of scope preferences which ascribes a central role to the projection of the Agreement System.
