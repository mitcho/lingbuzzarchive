Case and Agreement in a Corner of Icelandic
Norbert Hornstein
October 2018
 

This paper argues that the Anaphor-agreement effect (AAE) in Icelandic embedded non-finite clauses provides an argument in favor of the classical theory of case and against dependent case theory. In particular, the presence of AAE in embedded non finite clauses with Dative Subjects and Nominative objects can be explained if case is a head-DP relation (as in classical theory) but not if it is a DP-DP relation (as in dependent case theory).
