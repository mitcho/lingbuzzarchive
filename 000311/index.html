Eliminating Agree
Mike Putnam
May 2006
 

In this paper I examine the possibility of eliminating all non-local, look-back feature checking configuration from the narrow syntax. Based on Stroik's (2006) rigid revision of locality relations that define local relations strictly in Spec-Head configurations, I suggest that the Probe-Goal relation (i.e. Agree) as defined by Chomsky (2000 and later work) can be eliminated. To replace Agree, I introduce a new primative mechanism in the narrow syntax know as Link!
