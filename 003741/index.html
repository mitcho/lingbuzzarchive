Asymmetrical coordination. Syntax, semantics and pragmatics.
Hans Broekhuis
December 2018
 

This article will show that the special interpretations of asymmetrical coordinate structures result from an intricate interplay of syntax, semantics and pragmatics, but that there is no need to introduce special grammatical mechanisms or stipulations.
