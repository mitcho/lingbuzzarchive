The Syntax of 'Wherewithal'
Richard Kayne
April 2017
 

Sentences containing 'wherewithal' have a richer syntax than might at first glance be apparent. This richer syntax provides a handle on the absence of various potential words such as *whatwithal, *whenwithal, *herewithal, *therewithal, *wherewithwhole, *wherewithhalf, *wherewithalmostal, *wherewithboth that an approach associated with a less rich syntax could not provide.
