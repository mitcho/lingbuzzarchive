Domains for adverbs
Oystein Nilsen
August 2002
 

It is argued that many of the adverb ordering effects discussed most notably by Cinque (1999) can be derived from interaction of the semantics of the individual adverbs. In particular, it is argued that many adverbs are (positive) polarity items and that this suffices to derive a surprisingly large range of ordering effects.
