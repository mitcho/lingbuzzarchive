Prosodic Phrasing and Floating Numeral Quantifier Constructions in Scrambling
Gwangrak Son
January 2011
 

Although a number of challenges have been made to the distribution of FNQs in constructions of scrambling, many crucial questions are still unanswered. Giving special concerns to Miyagawa (1989, 2010), Ko (2007), and Miyagawa and Arikawa (2007), this paper fleshes out a new analysis termed as 'Restructuring', which plays a key role in understanding many questions remaining up to now. The process of 'xxxx ', motivated independently of syntactic operation, is important to reanalyze the whole set of data from a novel point of view. It will be shown that various types of problems detected in literature can be best explained by a conspiracy of the operations referring to the properties phonological as well as syntactic.
