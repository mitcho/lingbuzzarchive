Asymmetries in Latin glide formation
Kyle Gorman
May 2023
 

This paper considers whether Classical Latin glides /j, w/ are allophones of the high vowels /i, u/. Such an account is shown to be workable. A process of glide formation is involved in certain alternations in addition to allophony. It is shown that glide formation is asymmetrical, with front and back glides showing different behaviors in most contexts.
