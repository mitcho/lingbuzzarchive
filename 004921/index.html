Unifying Nominal and Verbal Syntax: Agreement and Feature Realisation
Peter Ackema, Ad Neeleman
December 2019
 

In this paper we argue, following Norris 2014, that concord is not agreement, but a feature realization mechanism triggered by the content of a dominating node. We discuss concord in both nominal and verbal domains, using data from Dutch, German, Archi and Gujarati.
