Determiner vs. adjectival numerals and nominal number
Roumyana Pancheva
April 2022
 

Borer (2005) identifies a link between the syntax of numerals and nominal number: only adjectival numerals, not determiner numerals, are compatible with singular-marked and dual-marked nouns. Schwarzschild (2006) reaches the same conclusion, on the basis of different premises. In this paper, I suggest that determiner numerals are able to combine with singular-marked nouns in principle, and restrictions on such combinations in some languages result from the inventory of the null measure expressions that are available as linkers between determiner numerals and nouns.
