Decomposing EPP effects in Greek enclisis
M M
November 2017
 

This paper discusses an extension condition imposed on TP/CP on the basis of Cypriot Greek and Standard Modern Greek enclisis. It proposes that this condition is subject to an Economy Principle satisfied at PF.
