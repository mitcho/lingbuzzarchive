Why more and less are never adverbs
Brett Reynolds
August 2023
 

The Cambridge grammar of the English language posits that while _more_ and _less_ are generally determinatives, they are adverbs in the context of analytic comparatives, such as in _more interesting_ or _less quickly_ because analytic more "does not enter into any [degree modifier] contrast with much."
