Sluicing and its subtypes
Luis Vicente
December 2014
 

This survey summarizes the accumulated evidence in favor of an articulate taxonomy of sluicing. An abridged version will appear in The Oxford Handbook of Ellipsis (eds. van Craenenbroeck & Temmerman), to be published in 2016.
