Reciclado y subversión gramatical. Sobre algunas variedades de recursos expresivos en español
Andrés Saab
April 2023
 

I discuss several case studies on Spanish expressivity that show that: (i) many forms of expressivity require what I call "grammatical recycling", which consists of reusing predicative-expressive words in non-predicative positions, both in syntax and morphology. The first data set show that grammatical deviation, in Corver's (2016) sense, is not the main device for generating expressive meanings in language. Other types of expressive devices, such as inclusive gender in Spanish, seem, instead, more amenable to a grammatical deviation approach.
