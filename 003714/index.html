Continuations for Comparatives
Todd Snider
March 2015
 

I extend Barker and Shan's (2014) project of finding natural language applications for continuations to another domain in which it is useful, namely comparatives. I introduce existing analyses of comparatives, in particular Heim 2000, 2006, and Schwarzschild and Wilkinson 2002 and then demonstrate how these analyses can be implemented in the continuations framework.
