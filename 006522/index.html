Historical Literary Languages
Keshav Ravi
March 2022
 

Latin, Greek, Sanskrit, and Arabic have each had an immense influence on the cultures around them. Whether by religion, war, or trade, they spread far past their own countries' borders. Here, I describe each language and its history, as well as how it affected languages around it.
