Natural Syntax: An Emergentist Primer (Chs. 1-9 only)
William O'Grady
October 2020
 

This short monograph draws on materials from my classes, talks and publications, as well as on new ideas that reflect my current thinking about language and emergence. It is intended for a general readership. This version of the manuscript contains just the first nine chapters. The entire monograph, consisting of 15 short chapters focused on the role of emergence in shaping language and its acquisition, is also available at LingBuzz.
