Shape Classifiers in Earlier Southern Min Texts
Chinfa Lien, Liching Livy Chiu
November 2013
 

This paper tackles shape classifiers in earlier Southern Min texts. It focuses on three kinds of shape classifiers, which collocate with noun phrases denoting specific sets of objects bearing common features. We flesh out the distributional pattern of each type of classifier and furnish a perceptual structure of shape classifiers based on a feature analysis. We also try to account for how less-straightforward shape classifiers function in terms of their etymological origin.
