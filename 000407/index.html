The syntax of Person, Tense, and speech features
Halldor Armann Sigurdsson
August 2004
 

The paper argues that Person interprets event participants in relation to speech participants in a parallel fashion as grammatical Tense interprets event time in relation to speech time. This understanding of Person is embedded in a general theory of speech-grammar-event matching, that is to say, an approach where speech (event) features, SF, are matched by grammatical features, GF, which in turn are matched by (propositional) event features, EF.
