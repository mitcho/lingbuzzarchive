Agreement in Germanic.
Halldor Armann Sigurdsson
March 2023
 

There are four major types of agreement in Germanic: finite verb agreement, primary predicate agreement, secondary predicate agreement, and DP-internal concord, and there is extensive variation among the Germanic languages across all these agreement phenomena. This work describes and discusses these Germanic agreement types.
