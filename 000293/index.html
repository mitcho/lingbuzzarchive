On Parameters and on Principles of Pronunciation
Richard Kayne
September 2005
 

I. The restriction of parametric variation to functional elements may be tenable despite a number of apparent counterexamples. II. The pronunciation or not of material that has a potential pronunciation may be entirely and automatically regulated by principles that make reference to phases and to spellout; spellout will have a 'blind spot' for material in the Spec of a phase.) If so, then UG has no need for an additional operation of deletion or ellipsis.
