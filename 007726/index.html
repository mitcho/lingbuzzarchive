The Ontology of Attitudinal and Modal Objects
Friederike Moltmann
November 2023
 

This is a prefinal version of Chapter 2 of Objects and Attitudes (Oxford UP forthcoming). It motivates and develops the ontology of attitudinal and modal objects as bearers of satisfaction conditions, in sharp contrast to the ontology of events.
