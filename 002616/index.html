Quantifier Domain Restriction as Ellipsis
Chris Collins
July 2017
 

In this paper I argue that in certain cases quantifier domain restriction is due to a process of ellipsis. I give evidence for this analysis from inverse scope phenomena. I respond to Stanley and Szabó’s arguments against a syntactic ellipsis approach. Furthermore, I show how their own semantic proposal is problematic and fails to capture the inverse scope facts.
