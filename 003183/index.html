If you P-have, you may give to [somebody]
Javier Ormazabal, Juan Romero
October 2016
 

Harley & Jung (2015) [H&J] argue that DOCs parallel the behavior of have in a systematic way with respect to a broad battery of phenomena, supporting a decompositional approach to double object give constructions. In this squib we show that for each test discussed in H&J, give+to passes the test like DOC. Applying their logic, this fact supports an analysis where have, give+DOC and give+to share a key (sub-)structure. We also argue that this fact has been obscured by the polysemy of send type verbs (Rappaport Hovav & Levin, 2008) [RH&L].
