Concentric Circles of Attachment in Pirah達
Daniel Everett
December 2013
 

This paper is not a linguistic paper per se, but it provides essential background to Pirah達 cultural learning and childhood development facts that underly the claim that culture can influence language/grammar. It is part of an on-going study on the internalization of cultural values, to appear as Everett, Daniel L. Dark Matter of the Mind, University of Chicago Press (2015).
