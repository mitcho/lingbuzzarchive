Two types of parasitic assimilation
Peter Jurgec
February 2013
 

This paper shows that consonant harmony and parasitic vowel harmony are more similar than previously assumed. I provide a unified and restrictive analysis of parasitic assimilation using feature spreading constraints. In particular, I attribute the differences between the attested and unattested patterns to two types of markedness constraintsâ€”alignment and agreement.
