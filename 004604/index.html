What's in a PreCure name?
Shigeto Kawahara
May 2019
 

This paper analyzes names in PreCure, a popular anime series in Japan, from the perspective of sound symbolism. It shows that labial consonants are overrepresented in the PreCure names compared to other name types. I argue that labial consonants symbolically represent cuteness and/or innocence, and that this sound symbolic principle is responsible for the distributional skew in the PreCure names. Other possible sound symbolic effects in PreCure names are also discussed.
