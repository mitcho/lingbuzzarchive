Instrument Subjects are Agents or Causers
Artemis Alexiadou, Florian Schaefer
July 2006
 

This paper focuses on instrument subjects. It shows that instruments are acceptable as subjects crosslinguistically under two interpretations, that of an Agent and that of a Causer. In doing this, the general point is that the distinction between the two theta roles Agent and Causer is syntactically reflected.
