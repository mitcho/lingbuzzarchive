On the difference between a √ and a root
Pavel Caha, Karen De Clercq, Guido Vanden Wyngaerd
June 2021
 

We propose a theory of root suppletion based on a single syntactic √ node. We show that for such a theory to be possible, a distinction must be made between syntactic √s and morphological roots. We argue against standard approaches to root suppletion like Harley (2014), Haugen and Siddiqi (2013), who differentiate √s in syntax.
