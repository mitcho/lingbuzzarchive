On One Reason for Not Deriving Kill from Cause to Die
Richard Dearmond
December 2009
 

I discuss one of Jerry Fodor's reasons not deriving KILL from CAUSE TO DIE. I support Fodor going further into the logical structure KILL and CAUSE TO DIE. Although his article helped the demise of generative semantics, it does not support Chomsky's theories of transformation grammar.
