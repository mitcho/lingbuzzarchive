Alienable-Inalienable Asymmetry in Japanese and Korean Possession
Tomoko Ishizuka
September 2009
 

This paper examines the distribution of alienable and inalienable possessors (i.e. possessors of Whole-Part possession) in Japanese and Korean relativization and topicailization and provides a syntactic analysis in the derivational theory. (Author's Version)
