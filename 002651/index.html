Negative Polarity Items in Ewe
Chris Collins, Paul Postal, Elvis Yevudey
August 2015
 

This paper investigates negation and negative polarity items (NPIs) in Ewe. First, we analyze Ewe ke-NPIs in terms of NEG raising. Second, we show that three parameters of variation distinguish English NPIs from Ewe NPIs. Third, we give an analysis of bipartite negation in Ewe that builds on our NEG raising analysis of Ewe NPIs. Fourth, we give a brief survey of non-ke-NPIs, and discuss how they fit into the analysis.
