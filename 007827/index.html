To the left, to the right, and much in between: A Festschrift for Katharina Hartmann
Anke Himmelreich, Daniel Hole, Johannes Mursell
January 2024
 

This collection combines various papers by linguistic companions of Katharina Hartmann. The Festschrift is published open-access as an e-book:
https://www.linguistik-in-frankfurt.de/festschrift-in-honour-of-katharina-hartmanns-60th-birthday/
