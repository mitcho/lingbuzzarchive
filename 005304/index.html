El problema de las alternancias argumentales en la teoría lingüística: el caso de la diátesis pasiva
Fernando Carranza
July 2020
 

In this thesis, I discuss different approaches to argument structure and argument alternations from a wide range of frameworks with special attention to passives. After an analysis of the way in which passive has been treated in the formal linguistics literature, I sketch a fragment of minimalist grammar, which aims to account for the syntax and semantic interpretation of passives.
