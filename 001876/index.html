Reasoning about equivalence in semantic fieldwork
Amy Rose Deal
August 2013
 

This paper discusses some of the hidden hypotheses that play a role in linguists' interpretation of native speakers' translations and judgments, and how these can influence the types of analyses linguists arrive at.
