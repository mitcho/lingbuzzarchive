Two Instances of a Minimalistic Syntactic Engine (Internal and External) are Responsible for A and A-bar Dependencies Respectively.
Sasson Margaliot
February 2017
 

I suggest a new architecture for syntax in which the workload is split between an Internal Syntactic Engine responsible for A-dependencies and an External Syntactic Engine responsible for A-bar dependencies. This approach re-introduces van Riemsdijk and Williams (1981) NP-structure in a minimalist setting.
