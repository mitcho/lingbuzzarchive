Syntax and the failure of analogical generalisation: A commentary on Ambridge (2020)
David Adger
January 2020
 

The syntactic behaviour of human beings cannot be explained by analogical generalization on the basis of concrete exemplars: analogies in surface form are insufficient to account for human grammatical knowledge, because they fail to hold in situations where they should, and fail to extend in situations where they need to.
