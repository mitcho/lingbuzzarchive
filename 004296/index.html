The nature of the phonology-syntax interface, from variable adjective and noun word order in Tagalog
Stephanie Shih, Kie Zuraw
November 2018
 

This manuscript is material from an earlier, unpublished version of Shih & Zuraw 2017 (in Language: Phonological Analysis) that did not appear in the published version after the final round of revisions, which requested that the paper shift its focus away from theoretical consequences for phonology-syntax interface design. Because the following discussion has been requested subsequently from readers of Shih & Zuraw 2017, we are making the material available.
