Complementizer agreement and the relation between T° and C°
Liliane Haegeman, Marjo van Koppen
December 2010
 

The goal of this paper is twofold. (i) We first provide empirical evidence to challenge the claims according to which there is a featural dependency between T° and C°. We will also briefly discuss some alternative proposals which also are shown to be empirically inadequate. (ii) We elaborate on West Flemish External Possessor Agreement (EPA), the pattern which is a cornerstone of our argumentation and which has hitherto not been observed or discussed in the literature.
