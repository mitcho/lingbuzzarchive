An Object-Based Tuthmaker Semantics for Modals
Friederike Moltmann
February 2020
 

This paper develops a new semantics of modals motivated by a greater range of linguistically reflected intuitions than generally taken account. The semantics is based on modal objects and their satisfiers and violators, rather than possible worlds.
