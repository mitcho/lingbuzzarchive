The layered structure of concord: A Nanosyntactic approach
Pavel Caha
December 2022
 

This paper discusses evidence for the claim that concord features on nominal modifiers do not form an unstructured bundle in the tree (occupying a single terminal), but exhibit rich internal structure, with each concord feature occupying a designated head in the morphosyntactic tree (Taraldsen 2010). The paper discusses concord in Dime, Finnish, Georgian, Khwarshi, Zulu.
