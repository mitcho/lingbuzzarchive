An Alternative Roadmap to Deep Linguistic Intelligence
Sasson Margaliot
May 2017
 

This article argues that for the success of real natural language processing it is necessary for three additional subsystems to be used in conjunction with Deep Learning: (a) extraction of features for Scientific Syntax, (b) ultra-simple pre-linguistic database (in which almost all ontological information should be placed), and (c) a database of presuppositions that reflects the multi-agent environment.
