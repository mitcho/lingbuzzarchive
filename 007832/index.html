The Syntax of Irish Gaelic
James McCloskey
January 2023
 

This paper attempts to offer an overview of the syntax of Irish and to one strand of research on Irish syntax. It was solicited for a handbook on the linguistics of the Celtic languages (in preparation) and is aimed at a general readership.
