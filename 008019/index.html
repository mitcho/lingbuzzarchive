On the structure of double-coordinator constructions
Metodi Efremov, Franc Lanko Marušič
April 2024
 

The paper presents novel Macedonian and Slovenian data with conjunction doubling that exhibit unexpected binding behaviour as each individual conjunct binds an anaphor in the object position individually. The paper argues these data cannot be simply explained away and proposes a silent quantifier in the structure of coordination.
