Reconstructing the A/A-distinction in reconstruction
Susi Wurmbrand
August 2009
 

This paper argues for the following claims: i) A-movement leaves a trace; ii) syntactic reconstruction is possible in A- and A-movement (incl. scrambling) contexts; iii) the possibility/distribution of reconstruction with scrambling is not determined by the A/A distinction, but instead by Economy consid-erations regulating interactions among LF, information structure, and PF.
