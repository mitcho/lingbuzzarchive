Greek subjunctive marker na: Temporal Anchoring in the DP domain
Eleni Staraki
February 2007
 

The goal of this paper is to propose an account for those cases where the na marker is prohibited although expected according to the semantics of the verb. We argue that na marker and other markers of the same category are not always crucial for the anchoring of the perfective non-past (PNP) dependent verb form in plain Free Relative (FR) structures. Finally it shows it is possible for time reference to come from other domains in the structure besides the CP domain.
