Verb Meaning and the Lexicon: A First Phase Syntax
Gillian Ramchand
March 2007
 

This is a monograph exploring an event-decompositional view of the first phase of the syntactic-semantic computation. It contains a decomposition of different verb types in English within the theoretical framework proposed, a chapter on resultatives and path of motion, and a chapter on causatives. The book has a substantial amount of cross-linguistic data, most notably from Bengali, Hindi/Urdu and Russian.
