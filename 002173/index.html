Denominal verbs: An overview
Andrew Mcintyre
August 2014
 

This article gives a summary of (the literature on) the syntactic, semantic and morphological aspects of the formation of denominal verbs (with and without affixes). It is posted here because it should be useful for people who want a quick overview of the subject, but parts of it might be of interest to linguists who have already worked on the subject. It tries to criticise all kinds of frameworks equally, so that it should be hard to guess the author's views on the subject.
