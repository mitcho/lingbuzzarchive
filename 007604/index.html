Scope of computational linguistics
Dr. Pandey Ashish
September 2023
 

The scope of Computational Linguistics with Artificial Intelligence (AI) is vast and promising. Computational Linguistics is the study of using computers to process and understand natural language, while AI focuses on developing machines and algorithms that can simulate human-like intelligence. When combined, these fields have the potential to revolutionize various aspects of communication, information processing, and human-computer interaction.
