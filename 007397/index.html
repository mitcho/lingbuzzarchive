Revisiting Basque (xe-)comparatives
Daniar Kasenov
July 2023
 

This paper presents a re-assessment of the allomorphy patterns found in Basque xe-comparatives and comparative adverbials (Bobaljik 2012). It is suggested that Basque xe-comparatives do not pose a problem for contemporary approaches to allomorphy based on adjacency once one accepts the split comparative hypothesis put forth by Caha, De Clercq, Vanden Wyngaerd (2019). Additionally, the paper presents a semantic treatment of the split comparative structure, building on Zhang and Ling's (2021) interval-based semantics for comparatives. Note: this is a first draft, proceed with caution.
