Compounds - the View from Hebrew
Hagit Borer
March 2008
 

This is in essence a short survey of compounds in Hebrew, and their similarities and differences with construct state nominals. Theoretically, the paper proposes a principled approach to the interaction between syntactic word formation and the emergence of non-compositional meaning, by defining, and narrowing down, the syntactic domain that can serve as an input to encyclopedic searches. A longer version is in the pipelines.
