Case as Agree Marker
Christer Platzack
June 2006
 

In this paper I suggest a partly new way to look at morphological case. The main part of the paper is an attempt to account for nominative, accusative and dative in modern Icelandic in terms of Agree-relations between T/v and DPs. The syntactic account presented here is an implementation of recent ideas within the Minimalist program (Chomsky, Pesetsky & Torrego), according to which the syntactic derivation is driven by features with the help of the operation Agree.
