Heavy Syllable Effects in Some Trochaic Syllable-counting Word-stress Systems
John Frampton
February 2024
 

The paper has two goals. First, it gives some evidence that the restrictive theory of word stress proposed in Frampton (2023) is empirically adequate. It also shows in some detail how that theory can succinctly capture the fine details of stress systems which result from the accumulation of residue from diachronic variation. Second, it demonstrates that the global maximization approach of OT fails to capture the directional iterative limited-lookahead character of rhythmic footing.
