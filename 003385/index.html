NEG-Raising in crosslinguistic perspective
Marie-Luise Popp
January 2016
 

A systematical investigation of the typological variation of NEG-Raising predicates in divergent languages provides new insights into the nature of NEG-Raising. I will show that the results of the study are potentially problematic for existing approaches to NEG-Raising.
