When and How Does Search Take Place?
Nobu Goto
May 2019
 

This squib proposes that a search procedure is involved not only in labeling the output of Merge{X, Y} (Label-Search) but also in determining the input of Merge(X, Y) (Input-Search), and the 3rd factor principle requires all search procedures not to access the deep interior of a complex structure such as {XP, YP}. It is shown that this proposal gives a unified account of so-called Pied-Piping and CED.
