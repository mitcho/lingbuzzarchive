Is there an LLM challenge for Linguistics? (or is there a Linguistics challenge for LLMs?) [slides]
David Pesetsky
October 2024
 

These are slides from a presentation on the topic of the title that I have given in various versions, starting with a computer science lunch at MIT in May 2023. This version was presented at the Romanian Academy of Sciences in May 2024. Perhaps I will write this up as an article in the future, but a number of people have found the slides themselves useful — especially for discussions with non-linguists — so I decided to post them here for the time being. Note the acknowledgment in slide 29 to my own summary paper on that-trace effects in the second edition of the Wiley Blackwell Companion to Syntax (also available on LingBuzz), from which much of the data in these slides is taken unchanged. Very little if anything in these slides will be new to knowledgable linguists (you have been warned!). The sole new point here is their relevance to the question asked in the title.
