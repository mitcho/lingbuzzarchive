Subjects across alignment systems
Hubert Haider
October 2022
 

They key for solving a series of self-inflicted problems in the comparative syntax of ergative languages is an empirically adequate concept of ‘subject’. An inapt understanding of ‘syntactic subject’ has led to avoidable confounds such as: no ergative SVO languages, no obligatory subjects in ergative languages, no object-to-subject promotion, etc.
