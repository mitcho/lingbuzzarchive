The discourse particle vallë in Albanian
Bujar Rushiti
August 2024
 

This paper investigates the discourse particle vall€e in Albanian. It provides the first in- depth description of the distribution and functional meaning of vall€e. I argue that vall€e is restricted to speech acts containing questions as opposed to assertions. The meaning of vall€e is expressive in nature since it contributes to the non-at-issue content of the utter- ance and expresses speaker's attitude of wondering towards the truth of the proposition. I provide an analysis in terms of Traugott's subjectification approach, according to which vall€e expresses the speaker's epistemic stance.
