Pronouns and their Antecedents
Richard Kayne
June 2001
 

A movement based approach to pronoun-antecedent relations can attributes Condition C and Condition B effects to properties of movement and eliminate both conditions as primitives of UG. The existence of reflexives is traceable back to Condition B. No use is made of `accidental coreference' in the sense of Lasnik (1976).
