Phase-Head Initiated Structure Building
Jae-Young Shim
November 2014
 

This paper explores an alternative structure building algorithm in minimalism. Along with other operations in Narrow Syntax (NS) such as Feature-Inheritance and Transfer, structure building is also initiated only by phase heads. Consequently, this paper takes one step further Chomskyâ€™s (2007, 2008, 2013) generalization that all operations in NS are restricted to the phase level. It also investigates the implications of phase-head initiated structure building regarding the motivations for Feature-Inheritance and Transfer (Chomsky 2007, 2008, Richards 2007). I argue that: 1) no derivation can converge at the C-I interface without the application of Feature-Inheritance, and 2) the operation Transfer is a natural by-product of (Internal) Merge.
