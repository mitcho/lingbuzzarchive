The Prosodic Licensing of Wh- In-situ: Evidence from Krachi and Wasa
Jason Kandybowicz, Harold Torrence
June 2015
 

Presented as a plenary talk at the 44th Annual Conference on African Linguistics, this paper evolved into the 2017 Linguistic Variation article “On Prosodic Variation and the Distribution of Wh– In-situ”. The article argues for a purely prosodic approach to deriving the variable distribution of wh- in-situ in the Tano languages Krachi and Wasa.
