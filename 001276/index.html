A Compositional Semantics for the Exceptive Modifier 'Else' in English
Matthew Barros
May 2011
 

A compositional analysis for 'else' in English indefinite pronoun constructions. 'Else' is analyzed as a head adjoined to D. Includes a stab at an account for 'else' modification in Wh-questions. For the most part the semantics for 'else' proposed in the earlier parts of the paper works for Wh-questions with 'else', though there are some unexpected properties which are discussed and left untreated.
