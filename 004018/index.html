Anaphora Resolution via Latent Antecedents Representation: A Brief Idea
Matteo Grella
May 2018
 

An idea is proposed to perform the task of anaphora resolution as a pointing problem (Lee et al., 2017) using the bidirectional recurrent autoencoders schema introduced by Grella and Cangialosi (2018).
