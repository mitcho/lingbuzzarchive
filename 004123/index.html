Formation of Western Romance Differential Object Marking (ad)
Keith Tse
November 2013
 

The wide geographical and historical distribution of Differential Object Marking (DOM) in Romance strongly suggests proto-Romance origins, and the Latin attestations of the allative preposition AD reveal a dual grammaticalization of AD as a case-marker of both direct and indirect objects, the former of which is subject to thematic and discursive differential marking which reveals further evidence of functionalist embedding within a formal (Minimalist) analysis of Case-marking (K(case)).
