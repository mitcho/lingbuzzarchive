The close connection between small seal script and the Bible
Yueqin Yang
August 2021
 

This paper studies seven groups of small seal script by analyzing the reasons for their creation, and finds that the logic of the small seal script in "Shuo Wen Jie Zi" actually is related to the Bible. In modern Chinese, the common meanings of these characters studied in this paper are based on their original meanings related to the Bible. The first group of characters studied in this paper are the six characters related to the character "Yin"[音]: "Zhang"[章], "Jing"[竟], "An"[暗], "Qi"[湆], "Yin"[喑] and "Yi"[意]. The second group of characters are the four characters related to the characters "Nv"[女] and "Kou"[口]: "Ru"[如], "Shu"[恕], "Ru"[挐], and "Ru"[帤]. The third group of characters are the seven characters related to the character "Tai"[台]: "Tai"[台], "Shi"[始], "Dai"[怠], "Yi"[眙], "Yi"[佁], "Hai"[咍], "Dai"[殆]. The fourth group of characters are the seven characters related to the character "che"[屮]: "che"[屮], "Mu"[木], "Zhu"[朱], "Ci"[朿], "Ji"[棘], "Zao"[棗] and "Di"[帝]. The fifth group of characters are the three characters related to the characters "Rou"[肉] and "Li"[力]: "Lei"[肋], "Jin"[筋], and "Xie"[脅]. The sixth group of characters are the eight characters: "Ren"[人], "Wu"[勿], "Yi"[易], "Xi"[蜥], "Shi"[睗], "Yi"[㑥], "Xi"[锡], "Yi"[晹]. The seventh group of characters are the three characters : "Niu"[牛], "Wu"[物], "Jian"[件].
