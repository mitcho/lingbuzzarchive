The Morphosyntax of Possessive Pronouns and Silent Nominals in Mongolian
Chigchi Bai
August 2024
 

This is the abstract of a talk presented at the Joint Conference of The Seoul International Conference on Generative Grammar (SICOGG26) and The Workshop on Altaic Formal Linguistics (WAFL18), held in Korea, Aug 22nd-24th, 2024. The paper studies the morphosyntax of Mongolian possessive pronouns within the framework of Distributed Morphology. The core proposal is that pronouns are not lexical items but rather feature clusters that are driven by syntactic operations and formalized by vocabulary insertion.
