Arity operations in Hindi: A Lexicon-Syntax Interface Perspective
Richa Srishti
November 2016
 

This paper is an attempt to probe into arity operations in Hindi from the perspective of Lexicon-Syntax interface. It provides evidence that though passivization in Hindi is not similar to its counterpart in English (where saturation of the external Î¸-role happens, i.e. canonical passive), it is a valid arity operation. It also tries to place the arity operations either in the syntax or in the lexicon.
