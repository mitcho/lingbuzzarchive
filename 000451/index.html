Review of Scheer (2004): A Lateral Theory of Phonology
Andrew Ira Nevins
June 2007
 

This review summarizes and discusses aspects of the theory of syllabification in Scheer (2004) and presents brief applications of the model to data in Brazilian Portuguese.
