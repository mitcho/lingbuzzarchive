Be careful where you transfer
Shin'ya Uchishiba
December 2022
 

In this paper, I investigate the nature of Transfer and its interplay with Labeling. The ultimate enterprise I aim at here is to lay out a parametric syntax on scrambling based on the Free-Merge model of grammatical architecture.
