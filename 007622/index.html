Microcontact and Syntactic Theory
Roberta D'Alessandro, Alberto Frasson, Manuela Pinto, Sorgini Luana, Silvia Terenghi
October 2023
 

This chapter presents an overview of the results of the Microcontact project, on heritage Italo-Romance languages in the Americas. It features four case studies, on auxiliary selection, null subjects and subject clitics, demonstratives, and differential object marking, and some discussion on the relevance of structural similarity as well as the importance of considering the microdimension in contact and heritage studies. This is a preprint version of the second draft. Comments, questions, feedback: very welcome!
