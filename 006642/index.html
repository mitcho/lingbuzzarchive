The syntax of mental imagery
Nishant Mulay
May 2022
 

This paper talks about the generation of mental imagery, which is assumed to be a kind of thought. The paper posits that mental imagery is formed in our minds using the generative procedure that primarily generates sentences. Essentially speaking, generative syntax that produces sentences is also used for the generation of mental imagery.
