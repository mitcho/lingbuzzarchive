Splitting the Nominal Left Periphery into DP and TopP: Two Pre-adjectival Positions for Possessives in German
Dorian Roehrs
October 2018
 

This paper argues that the nominal left periphery in German is complex in that there must be another phrasal level above the DP-layer. Arguing that Possessor Doublings like Peter sein as in Peter sein Auto ‘(Peter his =) Peter’s car’ and Saxon Genitives like Peters as in Peters Auto ‘Peter’s car’ surface in Spec,DP, it is proposed that von-possessives like von Peter as in von Peter das Auto ‘(of Peter the =) Peter’s car’ are in Spec,TopP.
