Donum semanticum: Opera linguistica et logica in honorem Barbarae Partee a discipulis amicisque Rossicis oblata
Peter Arkadiev, Ivan Kapitonov, Ekaterina Rakhilina
June 2015
 

This is a collection of articles in honor of Barbara Hall Partee written by her friends, colleagues and former students from Russia, where Barbara has taught for almost twenty years. Edited by Peter Arkadiev, Ivan Kapitonov, Yury Lander, Ekaterina Rakhilina & Sergei Tatevosov.
