The Role of Culture in the Emergence of Language
Daniel Everett
May 2014
 

This paper builds on recent research on the effects of culture on the emergence of language, examining the link between culture, evidentiality, and syntax in Pirahã on the one hand and segmental phonology and culture on the other. It argues that culture is causally implicated in I-language and core grammar.
