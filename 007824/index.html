'Intelligent design' of grammars – a result of cognitive evolution
Hubert Haider
January 2024
 

The grammars of human languages are outcomes of Darwinian evolution (= variation + selection) applying at the level of a cognitive system. The UG-based approach amounts to "intelligent design".
