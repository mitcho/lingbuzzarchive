On the Syntax of Quantity in English
Richard Kayne
June 2002
 

In all languages, modifiers with the interpretation of 'many' or 'few' necessarily modify either silent NUMBER (or overt 'number'). UG imposes a maximum of one interpretable syntactic feature per lexical item (Principle of Decompositionality).
