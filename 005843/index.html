How Pokémonastics has evolved
Shigeto Kawahara
April 2021
 

Pokémonastics is a research paradigm in which we use Pokémon names to study the nature of sound symbolic patterns in natural languages. In this document, I would like to look back at how this project has developed so far, highlighting the contributions of my collaborators. I hope to keep updating this document, as we complete more studies within Pokémonastics. A related YouTube lecture can be found here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fcEE5aaVRcA&t=12s
