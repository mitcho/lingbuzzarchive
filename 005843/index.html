How Pokémonastics has evolved
Shigeto Kawahara
January 2023
 

Pokémonastics is a research paradigm in which researchers use Pokémon names to explore the nature of sound symbolic patterns in natural languages. In this document, I would like to review how this project has developed so far, highlighting the contributions of my collaborators. I hope to keep updating this document, as we complete more case studies within Pokémonastics.
