Why Phi?
David Adger, Daniel Harbour
September 2006
 

This is the introduction to a collection of papers on Phi features in morphology, syntax and semantics. It provides a brief synoptic overview of how phi-features have been treated in these various domains of grammar, and what connections can be made between these treatments.
