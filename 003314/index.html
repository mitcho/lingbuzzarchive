Probing the nature of the Final-over-Final Constraint: the perspective from adpositions
Theresa Biberauer
January 2016
 

This paper focuses on superficially FOFC-violating PP-structures of various kinds, arguing that both their distribution and their internal make-up point to a characterisation of FOFC that references extended projections (as in Biberauer, Holmberg, & Roberts 2014, Biberauer in press).
