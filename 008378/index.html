Animacy. Lessons from Russian [Одушевленность. Русский язык]
Anton Zimmerling
August 2024
 

This paper is addressed the animacy feature in the Russian grammar, with focus on Russian verbal impersonal constructions and copular structures with non-verbal predicatives. I argue that animacy as the classifying category of Russian nouns (ANIM I) is a scalar feature, while animacy as the restrictive condition blocking ill-formed combinations of syntactic elements in Russian grammatical constructions (ANIM II) is construed as a discrete binary feature.
