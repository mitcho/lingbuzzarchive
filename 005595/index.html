Voynich codex; the hidden language
Pardis Motiee
December 2020
 

Like a chemical equation which the form present in both sides is removed to get the general reaction, in order to find out Voynich's true language the repetitive parts have to be removed, and it has to be done with certain rules. In this writing, by examining a paragraph of the manuscript, I will show how this process works and finally compare the results with plant illustration.
