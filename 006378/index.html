Über „über“ und dessen Entsprechungen im Englischen
Simon Masloch
June 2020
 

This Master’s thesis presents a corpus of German-English sentence pairs where the German sentence contains the preposition "über". The annotations are used to create mappings between the different readings of "über" and its English translation equivalents. It is shown that—with the exception of "over"—each of the translation equivalents is only used with a small subset of the readings of "über". The usefulness of the annotations and the mappings for a variety of different purposes, including a semantic description of über, word-sense disambiguation, the creation of bilingual dictionaries, and the search for expressions with a specific meaning is demonstrated. The thesis is written in German.
