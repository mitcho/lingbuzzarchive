A Very Short Note on Half vs. All
Richard Kayne
July 2022
 

Against the background of certain similarities between 'half' and 'all', a certain difference between them having to do with stranding can be understood in terms of a silent OF needed with 'half' (in the absence of an overt 'of'), but not with 'all'. The account appears to fit well with the stranding analysis of Sportiche (1988).
