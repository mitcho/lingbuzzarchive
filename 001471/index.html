Theoretical Approaches to Disharmonic Word Order
Theresa Biberauer, Michelle Sheehan
January 2013
 

This is a broad introduction to the topic of disharmonic word order in linguistic theory. It is also the introduction to a volume on the topic edited by the authors which is currently under review.
