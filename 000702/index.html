What Aspect can tell us about the future of MUST
Marika2 Lekakou, Oystein Nilsen
June 2008
 

We sketch a semantic derivation of the coocurrence constraints on types of modals (epistemic/root) and TMA marking on the embedded verb (perfective non-past/imperfective non-past) in Greek. Universal epistemic modals can't embed a perfective non-past, while existential epistemics can, as can root modals (universal or existential).
