Phi-feature agreement in syntax
Omer Preminger
April 2021
 

A very short piece (6.5 pages of text, along with about 4 pages of references) outlining what I take to be the current state of play with respect to the theory of phi-feature agreement.

(If you are interested in the historical developments that led us to where we are now, I strongly recommend Roberta D'Alessandro's contribution to the same handbook.)
