VP is elided during the course of derivation
Dongwoo Park
April 2021
 

In this squib, I first introduce a novel paradox based on affixation facts found in VPE and ellipsis generating reprise fragments (RFs). Then, I argue this paradox can be straightforwardly resolved if VPE occurs during the course of derivation, while ellipsis generating RFs (ERF) does so at PF, under the assumption that it is at morphology that inflection occurs
