English Intonational Patterns: an Autosegmental - Metrical Approach in Optimality Theory.
Cosme R.
September 2013
 

ABSTRACT The purpose of this article is to present a few intonational patterns of the english language. Based on the Intonational Model of Autosegmental – Metrical Theory (Ladd, 1996) in the framework of Optimality Theory (Prince&Smolensky, 1993) we intend to capture some of the constraint ranking (the grammar) of these intonational patterns.
