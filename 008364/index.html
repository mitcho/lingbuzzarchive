Dimensions of morphological complexity
Stephen R. Anderson
February 2015
 

My goal in this chapter is to develop and elaborate a characterization of the morphological characterization of languages along lines similar to Sapir’s, so as not only to serve similar typological goals but also to provide a framework for understanding the questions of linguistic typology
