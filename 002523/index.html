Abruzzese complementizers in diachrony
Roberta D'Alessandro, Claudio Di Felice
June 2015
 

Complementizer systems in Southern Italian Dialects (SIDs) display a certain degree of complexity. This paper documents the evolution path of complementizers in a southern Italian variety: Abruzzese. This is a first draft: comments are very welcome!
