Subject Blocking in Verbal Idioms: Evidence from Passive-Like Constructions in Icelandic
Anton Ingason, Einar Sigur√∞sson, Jim Wood
June 2016
 

This paper examines passive-like constructions in Icelandic and argues that idioms cannot be interpreted via traces and that the loss of idiomatic interpretation under passivization depends on the availability of displacement. We develop a mechanism of Late Transfer of Idioms which accounts for the observed facts.
