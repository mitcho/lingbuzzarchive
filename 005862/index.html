Ende
Kate Lindsey
April 2021
 

This paper is an illustration of the phonetics and phonemics of Ende, a Pahoturi River (Papuan) language of southern Papua New Guinea. Special issues include the phonemic status of prenasalization, the retroflex and liquid inventory, and the morphologically glossed text.
