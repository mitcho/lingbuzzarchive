Mandarin Data without a real analysis (I): Predicate Types in Relative Clauses and the Complementizer Deletion
Xuan Di
January 2009
 

Call de a complementizer in Mandarin relative clauses. Its well known that de can be deleted when the head noun is preceded by a demonstrative. However, the complementizer deletion is not just conditioned on the complexity of the head noun, even when the head noun is a demonstrative phrase, if the predicate of the relative clause is resultative or 'le' suffixed, the complementizer deletion cannot be licensed.
