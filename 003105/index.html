The canonical goal passive in Dutch and its dialects
Liliane Haegeman
August 2016
 

This paper challenges claims in the recent literature that Dutch lacks a canonical goal passive. Evidence is provided from West Flemish, a Dutch dialect, for the existence of goal passives. Following standard diagnostics it is shown that the goal argument can be promoted to subject in the passive. The final part of the paper raises the possibility that the availability of the goal pssive is not restricted to West Flemish.
