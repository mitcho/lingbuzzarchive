D, T, C drop in heritage Italo-Romance in NYC. Some descriptive generalizations
Luigi Andriani, Roberta D'Alessandro
December 2022
 

This paper reports some findings on the status of determiners, tense and complementizers in a number of heritage Italo-Romance varieties, including Italian, in New York City. Through extensive fieldwork we identified some clear tendency to drop the D head, and some drop/impoverishment of T and C. The contexts in which these droppings happen are not always coincident with any allowed structure of the contact languages. Moreover, we present some data on the lack of verb movement in heritage varieties. Comments/questions/feedback very welcome!
