'Movement', precedence, c-command
Michael Brody
May 2018
 

An improved account of how the core properties of movement, c-command and precedence, follow without any movement-specific stipulations and without assuming strong antisymmetry, -- essentially from the general restrictive assumption that syntax generates one-dimensional objects.
