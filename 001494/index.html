Clitic left dislocation as agreement -- a movement approach
Mikko Kupula
April 2012
 

The paper investigates Clitic Left Dislocation (CLLD) in Modern Greek as a morphological phenomenon. The "clitic" in CLLD is a spellout of DO-agreement features. The paper also shows the superiority of a movement approach to CLLD (rather than a base-generation analysis). Comments welcome!
