Dutch linguistics and the general audience
Marc Van Oostendorp
March 2019
 

This very brief paper gives an overview of outreach activities of Dutch linguists since 1928, with a focus on the present time. It shows how the circumstances for such activities are relatively positive, although of course nothing is perfect.
