Slavic Obviative Subjunctives
Petr Biskup
June 2021
 

In this article, I investigate Slavic obviative subjunctives embedded under the volitional verb ‘want’. I propose an analysis which is based on the operation Upward and Multiple Agree and which uses a quasi imperative operator. In contrast to most previous approaches, the proposal can also derive weakened obviation effects.
