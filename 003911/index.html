Incremental Topological Sorting for Phonological Learning
Yu Cao
November 2017
 

This paper has reformulated one of the most basic tasks in phonological learning, that of finding a (stratified) linear order compatiblewith given ERCs as disjunctive topological sorting. It has thereby paved the way for incorporating the idea of selective updating from recent developments in online topological sorting to deal with the incremental nature of phonological learning.
