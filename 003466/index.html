Upward-Oriented Complementizer Agreement with Subjects and Objects in Kipsigis
Michael Diercks, Meghana Rao
May 2017
 

In Kipsigis (Nilotic, Kenya), declarative-embedding complementizers can agree with both main-clause subjects and main-clause objects. This paper documents the morphosyntactic patterns of both, as well as describing the interpretive consequences of both agreement relations.
