Spanish usted as an Imposter
Chris Collins, Francisco Ordóñez
December 2020
 

Across dialects, Spanish uses the third person forms usted and ustedes to refer to the addressee. In this squib, we propose an imposter analysis of these forms in the framework of Collins and Postal 2012.
