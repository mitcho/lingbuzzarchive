The Nominal Left Periphery in German: Abstract and Overt Agreement Chains
Dorian Roehrs
April 2019
 

This paper provides a new explanation of why German alle seine Freunde 'all his friends' is grammatical but alle Peters Freunde 'all Peter's friends' is not. It also discusses the contrast between the grammatical alle diese Freunde 'all these friends' and the marked alle die Freunde 'all the friends'.
