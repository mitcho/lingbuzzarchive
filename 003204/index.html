Dogs process associations, not lexical or prosodic information
Marc Hauser, Robert Berwick, Jeffrey Watumull, Noam Chomsky
November 2016
 

Andics et al use fMRI data to claim that dogs perform lexical and prosodic computations, concluding that these capacities evolved in the absence of language. The evidence does not support these conclusions.
