Missing Objects in Persian
Vahideh Rasekhi
October 2016
 

This paper investigates missing objects in Persian ellipsis structures. I argue that missing objects in structures with simple predicates involve Definite DP (DDP) ellipsis. I provide evidence that the verb-stranding verb phrase ellipsis analysis proposed for Persian complex predicates (Toosarvandani 2009) cannot account for such constructions. In addition, I show that DDP ellipsis is licensed by V and the data shed some light on the structural identity requirement in ellipsis.
