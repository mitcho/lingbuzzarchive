Alleged Broad Subjects in Hebrew: A Rejoinder to Doron & Heycock (2010)
Idan Landau
April 2010
 

In response to Doron & Heycock (2010), this rejoinder reaffirms the conclusion of Landau (2009) that Hebrew has no Broad Subject construction. Both in distribution and interpretation, the alleged broad subject patterns with left-dislocated phrases and not with standard subjects. Doron & Heycocks counterarguments are either inconclusive or confounded by independent factors.
