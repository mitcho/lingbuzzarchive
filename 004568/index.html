Leveraging monolingual developmental techniques to better understand heritage languages
Lisa Pearl
April 2019
 

In their keynote article, Polinsky and Scontras highlight intriguing behavioral similarities between heritage language use and monolingual development. Given this, I wonder if we can leverage existing comparative, behavioral, and computational approaches that are currently deployed to understand monolingual development in order to better understand heritage languages. I sketch out a few instances of each developmental approach, based on the concrete examples Polinsky and Scontras discuss of heritage language use.
