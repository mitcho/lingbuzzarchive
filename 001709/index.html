A Língua Pirahã e a Teoria da Sintaxe
Daniel Everett
January 1990
 

This is the first grammar of the Piraha language, a 1983 ScD dissertation at the University of Campinas, Brazil. The principal changes in my analysis over the years have been the interpretation of a few lexemes, the verbal morphology, and the functions of the morpheme -sai. A new grammar of Piraha, dictionary, and searchable data base of all data collected by me should begin in 2013, aiming for publication and web-availability in 2017.
